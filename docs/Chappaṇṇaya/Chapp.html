Chappaṇṇayagāhāō

nijjariya-jarā-maraṇaṁ vandittā jiṇa-varaṁ mahāvīraṁ

chappaṇṇaya-gāhāo vocchaṁ suyaṇassa joggāo

purise suyaṇe sacca-samiddhe, aliya- dalidde pamukke sahāve-santuṭṭhe

tava- niyama-dhamma-maïe dhamma-ṇiyama-maïe niyama-sīlakalie visamā vi gahā dasā samā honti

gayaṇammi gahā sayaṇammi sumiṇayā suviṇayā suvayaṇā saüṇayā vaṇaggesu

taha vāharanti purisaṁ jaha diṭṭhaṁ puvva-kammehiṁ

savve tāha pasatthā sumiṇā saüṇā saüṇā sumiṇā gahā ya nakkhattā

ti-huyaṇa-maṅgala-nilayaṁ hiyaeṇa jiṇaṁ vahantassa

kattha ï na karaï na karei na lahei mollaṁ suvaṇṇayaṁ kattha dūhavo vando

kattha ï va sarala-sahāvo jaṇo na saṁ u ava māṇaṇaṁ lahaï

dūra-ttho ḍahaï ravī, aggī aṅgehĩ pharasio pharusio ḍahaï

ḍahaï piya-vippaogo, suyaṇo vi a-daṁ a-ddaṁ saṇe ḍahaï

ḍahae suyaṇa-viogo cintā ḍahati sarīraṁ ḍahaï ya suyaṇa-viogo ḍahadi piya-vippayogo , ḍahaï ḍahadi a-ṇāhattaṇaṁ para-viese

ḍahaï ḍahadi ya abbhakkhāṇaṁ, ḍahaï ḍahadi a- kajjaṁ kicchaṁ kayaṁ kadaṁ pacchā

santa-guṇa-vippaṇāse asanta-dosubbhave ya vi jaṁ dukkhaṁ

taṁ sosei samuddaṁ; kiṁ puṇa hiyayaṁ maṇussāṇaṁ?

santehi a-santehi ya parassa kiṁ jampiyehi dosehiṁ?

attho jaso na labbhaï appā paḍaï kilese , so ya vi hu a-mitto kao hoi

mahureṇa jiṇaha kaḍuyaṁ, khamāĕ dappaṁ guruṁ paṇāmeṇa

santoseṇa ya lohaṁ lobhaṁ , kalahaṁ ca ajampiyavveṇa

ekka-sukaeṇa tūsaha jāva sayaṁ dukkiyāṇa volei

sa-sarīre vi na sakkā diyahe diyahe piyaṁ kāuṁ

kaha nāma tassa pāvaṁ cintijjaï āgae vi āgayammi kohammi kovammi

uppala campaya -dala-sukumālo jassa ghare ŏllio allio hattho?

piya-pāhuṇa-āgamaṇe a-pahuppantassa appa-dupaṁtassa sajjaṇa-jaṇassa

maraṇāo abbhahiyaṁ garuyayaraṁ uppajjaï māṇasaṁ māṇusaṁ dukkhaṁ

na hu aï-bhariyaṁ dhammaï dhamme ; jaha bhariyaṁ taha suhāvahaṁ hoi;

pancammi na ya kilimmaï kilissaï gao vi piya-pāhuṇo hoi.

te dhannā je dhammaṁ suṭṭhu vi jāṇanti suṭṭhu vi karanti karenti

jīviya-sāram a-sāraṁ: khaṇeṇa pariyattae savvaṁ suṇamāṇā pecchamāṇā ya

sā māyā so ya piyā, taṁ mittaṁ bandhavo ya bandhavo vi so ceva

jo hiya-nissesa-karaṁ dhammassa pahe nayaṁ dei

na vi taṁ karei māyā piyā ya putto ya bandhava-jaṇo ya

jaṁ purisassāyariyā karanti dhammaṁ uvaïsantā.

kiṁ acchaha vīsatthā? diyahā volenti turiya-pahiya vva;

te ya na gaṇanti tubbhe, tubbhehi na vi ya gaṇijjanti.

tuṭṭhā honti maṇussā: Indamaho āgao tti ramaṇīo;

na vi jāṇanti varāyā: paḍiyā saṁvacchara-salāyā!

uccāsaṇo mah-iccho, āṇāinto ya nir-uvayārīo

alaso piya-jimmaṇa rūsaṇo ya na ciraṁ pio hoi.

sahasatti pīimantā a-tthakke jaṁ va taṁ va jampantā

pāse ya nivesantā piyā ya piyāya sattuttaṇam uventi.

nīyaṁ aïkkamanto puvvābhāsī piyāĩ jampanto

chandaṁ aṇuyattanto veso vi jaṇo piyo hoi.

chandaṁ jo aṇuyattaï mammaṁ rohaï guṇā payāsei

na hu navari māṇusāṇa ya, devāṇa vi dullaho hoi.

āvaṁ ti ya evaṁ ti ya vottavvaṁ paṇḍieṇa puriseṇa:

iyaro vi hoi tuṭṭho, iyaraṁ pi (ya) tattiyaṁ ceva.

chajjaï pahussa laliyaṁ, piyāĕ māṇaṁ, khamā samatthassa

jāṇantassa ya bhaṇiyaṁ, moṇaṁ ca a-yāṇamāṇassa.

sahaï na kaḍuyaṁ jampaï, dose na lahaï guṇā payāsei

rūsantāṇa na rūsaï, dakkhiṇṇa-mahābhaḍo suyaṇo.

suyaṇo na ceva rūsaï: aha rūsaï mangulaṁ na cintei:

aha cintei na jampaï; aha jampaï lajjio hoi.

jaṁ dujjaṇassa suyaṇo lajjāe sahaï chinna-lajjassa

taṁ jāṇaï nillajjo, eso a-samatthao sahaï.

aṇuyattiyaṁ na yāṇaï, piye vi bhaṇie vi a-ppiyaṁ bhaṇaï,

dāṇeṇa na sampajjaï; natthi uvāo khala-yaṇassa.

bajjhaï mantehi visaṁ, hatthi rajjūhi seuṇā salilaṁ;

payaï-visamassa bandho na yāṇimo dujjaṇa-jaṇassa.

savvassa esa payaī: piyammi uppāie piyaṁ kāuṁ:

suyaṇassa kā vi payaī: a-kae vi pie piyaṁ kāuṁ.

kajjammi samāvaḍie aṇuyattaha dujjaṇaṁ payatteṇa

visam āsā kira jāyaï amayāŏ visaṁ na saṁbhavaï.

jaï vi khamā paribhūyā, jaï vi khamantassa āyaro natthi

taha vi khamā khamiyavvā; khama-sariso bandhavo natthi.

kaha tassa rūsiyavvaṁ jassa paṇāmo puṇo vi kāyavvo?

accanta-nigguṇassa vi kaha sakkā rūsiuṁ tassa?

rosassa khamā daṇḍo, kāma-guṇāṇaṁ nirumbhaṇā daṇḍo;

dujjaṇa-jaṇassa kalah-ujjayassa parivajjaṇā daṇḍo.

bhaddaṁ pahū suyāṇaṁ avarāheūṇa baddha-vaïrāṇa

paṇivaïya-vacchalāṇaṁ muhutta-caṇḍ-ĕkka-rosāṇaṁ.

bīheyavvaṁ dujjaṇa-jaṇassa niccaṁ paloṭṭa-jīhassa

vara-rayaṇa-nijjharassa va vayaṇa-karālaṁ vahantassa.

atthakkŏ jampae payaï-pāyaḍaṁ savva-loya-tattillaṁ;

taṁ māṇusa-veyālaṁ puhaī vi sa-sankiyā bhamaï.

nīo khaṇeṇa raccaï; ratto santo viraccaï khaṇeṇa,

nīo viratta-metto bhāmei gharā-ghariṁ dose.

suyaṇo cirassa raccaï; ratto santo viraccaï cirassa;

suyaṇo jaï vi viraccaï; taha vi na dose payāsei.

chullucchalei jaṁ hoi ūṇayaṁ rittayaṁ kaṇayaṇei;

bhariyāī na vuccanti supurisa-viṇṇāṇa-bhaṇḍāīṁ.

taṁ khāha jaṁ jarijjaï, taṁ jampaha jaṁ mahā-yaṇe joggaṁ;

taṁ jampanti su-purisā, kāleṇa vi jaṁ na langhei.

tattiya-mettaṁ jampaha jettiya-mettassa nikkalaṁ cavaha jattiya-mettassa nikkayaṁ vahaha

taṁ ukkhiveha ukkhaveha bhāraṁ jaṁ addha-pahe na chaḍḍeha.

vujjhanti nāma bhārā, te cciya vujjhanti vīsamantehiṁ

sīla-bharo voḍhavvo jāvaj-jīvaṁ a-vīsāmo avissāmo .

jo sakkayaṁ na yāṇaï suvisuddha-pāiyaṁ pi vottuṁ-je

moṇaṁ tu va tassa saraṇaṁ, nīsaraṇaṁ ahava parisāe.

uccaṁ na sikkhio addha-sikkhio sikkhio ku-sālāe

jo vahaï viusa-vāyaṁ so sariso sanniyāeṇa.

ko kuvalayāṇa gandhaṁ karei mahurattaṇaṁ ca ucchūṇaṁ

vara-hatthīṇa ya līlaṁ, viṇayaṁ ca kula-ppasūyāṇaṁ?

aṇuyattaha bhaṇaha piyaṁ, jaṁ kajjaṁ taṁ muṇeha suṇeha hiyaeṇa:

mahuraṁ lavaï mayūro sa-visaṁ ca bhuyangamaṁ gilaï.

ucchū mahuro somo cando (taha) candaṇaṁ ciya su-surabhiṁ

abhijāo ya kulīṇo; ko doso āyaro c'eva?

jo guḍa-kayāĕ vāyāĕ jampae visa-kaeṇa hiyaeṇa

so bila-gao va sappo vajjeyavvo payatteṇa

a-ddiṭṭhe vi khala-yaṇe nāma-ggahaṇe vi hoi āsankā

aliya-palatte vi ahī ahi tti bhaṇa ko na bīhei?

saṇḍeva-paḍiya-paccuṭṭhieṇa sīeṇa tharaharanteṇa

thereṇa pahe runnaṁ tāruṇṇaṁ sambharanteṇaṁ.

vivarīyā thera-maī vārijjantī ya jāi thera-maī

kālo tettiya-thāmaṁ na pecchase paṇḍuraṁ sīsaṁ.

taṁ hi sarīraṁ taṁ ji muhu, tāī ji lōyaṇayāĩ

nāi vihī sīsaṁ caraï, dinnau pāu jarāĕ.

taĩ pahu dhaṇiya ghareṇa gharu nayaṇihĩ dāiya sō hu;

ēvahi jara-nīhōḍiyau, paḍijiya acchaï ēhu.

laṭṭhī-dhariya-kaḍevara gaya-jovaṇa evam eva santuṭṭha;

sahaï kaḍuyaṁ pi bhaṇio: gao tumaṁ rūsiuṁ kālo!

siru paliyaũ muhũ vali-bhariu; jara laggī haya nāma

maraṇaṁ hōi acintiyaũ; dhammu karahi jiya nāma.

caya mahilaṁ muha-kaḍuyaṁ, caya mittaṁ natthi jattha sab-bhāvaṁ

caya sāmiyaṁ a-dāyaṁ, cayāhi paribhūyā vāsaṁ ca.

ālattaṁ ciya rūsaï, bhiuḍiṁ kāūṇa dei ullāvaṁ

mahilā-chaleṇa purisaṁ bāhei purāṇayaṁ kammaṁ.

mahilāṇa esa payaī: savvassa karenti vemaṇassāiṁ;

tassa na karenti navaraṁ jassa alaṁ kāmayanteṇaṁ.

piya-jaṇa-apatta-bhogā bālattaṇayammi gahiya-sāmaṇṇā

a-ddiṭṭha-piya-viogā jiyanti muṇiṇo suhaṁ loe.

su-labho vimāṇa-vāso, ega-cchattā ya meiṇī su-labhā;

logammi navari du-lahā pāyā jiṇa-vaddhamāṇassa.

ucchū sahāva-mahuro, nimbo kaḍuo huyāsaṇo ḍahaṇo

suyaṇassa dujjaṇassa ya payaï-viseso sahāveṇa.

jaṁ uppalassa patteṇa chijjae kiṁ va sattha-maggeṇaṁ?

mahurammi bhāṇiyavve, kiṁ te kaḍueṇa bhaṇieṇa?

mahura-vayaṇehĩ āsāsiellayā je gayā para-viesaṁ

te tāĩ sambharantā, annattha raïṁ na bandhanti.

kaḍuya-vayaṇehĩ nibbhacchiyallayā chinna-pema-sambandhā

te tāĩ sambharantā, tatto ya pareṇa pare ṇa vaccanti.

dhannāṇa hiyaṁ ca miyaṁ ca gāhayaṁ desa-kāla-bhaṇiyaṁ ca

na ya para-loya-viruddhaṁ guṇa-pattaṭṭhaṁ bhaṇaï jīhā.

esā satti-pahāre ya kasa-pahāre ya bala-pahāre ya

ucchahaï davāveuṁ du-viṇīyā attaṇo jīhā.

jo dujjaṇeṇa mettī karei suyaṇo tti nāma kaliūṇa

suviṇaya-laddho va nihī pacchā icchaṁ na pūrei.

dujjaṇa-saṁsaggīe āvāe ucchu-khaṇḍa-mahurāe;

visam iva aṇāya- giliyaṁ gīyaṁ pacchā hoī a-patthāo.

jaï kancaṇaṁ girīo jāyaï (jaï) (mottiyaṁ) ca hattīo

viṇao ya kulīṇāo, ko doso āyaro ceva?

paḍivayaṇa-samattheṇaṁ sahiyavvaṁ dujjaṇassa suyaṇaeṇa:

na ya paṭṭhimmi nilīṇo vasahassa siriṁ haraï kāo.

ekko khamāĕ doso savvālankāra-bhūsiya-guṇāe

a-samattho tti gaṇijjaï jaṁ puriso dujjaṇa-jaṇeṇa.

tisu je a-diṭṭha-puvvā rāya-kule guru-kule ya vesāsu

viṇṇāṇa-māṇa-laḍahattaṇāṇa te bāhirā honti.

nāūṇa ya viṇṇāṇaṁ kāūṇa ya samara-sāhasaṁ kammaṁ

langheūṇa ya Meruṁ puṇṇehĩ viṇā suhaṁ katto?

kāle uvaṇijjantī thevā vi mahosahī jiyāvei:

amaya-rasa-mīsieṇa vi mayassa kiṁ lāvaya-raseṇa?

nhāyassa ya dhovattī jimiyassa ya jaṁ ca taṁ ca tambolaṁ

avasara-paḍhiyā gāhā, tinni vi saggaṁ visesanti.

nīyaṁ ca laddha-pasaraṁ sippi-pauttaṁ ca attha-saṁjuttaṁ

mahilāyattaṁ ca gharaṁ, tinni vi sūlaṁ visesanti.

thakke bhaṇiyaṁ sohaï, thakke bhaṇiyaṁ suhāvahaṁ hoi;

a-tthakke puṇa bhaṇiyaṁ kayaṁ pi kajjaṁ viṇāsei.

kiṁ tammeha palāyaha jaṁ saraṇaṁ natthi ti-huyaṇe sayale

mottuṁ jiṇenda-vayaṇaṁ, sesaṁ savvaṁ kalayalei.

suyaṇo sarala-sahāvo maïlijjanto vi dujjaṇa-jaṇeṇa

chāreṇa dappaṇo vviya ahiyayaraṁ nimmalo hoi.

suyaṇassa dujjaṇārūsiyassa kaḍuyāĩ bhannamāṇassa

kamala-maule vva bhamaro bāhira-hiṇḍī bhamaï roso.

jaha jaha aṇuyattijjaï taha taha nīyattaṇassa doseṇa

vaṭṭā-dhūlō va khalo caḍaï sire natthi sandeho.

jeṇa viṇā na jivijjaï aṇuṇijjaï so kayāvarāho vi:

patte ya nagara-dāhe bhaṇa kassa na vallaho aggī?

taṁ jampanti su-purisā taṁ ciya pamuhe karenti paḍivattī

rayaṇāyaro vva velaṁ kāleṇa vi jaṁ na langhei.

para-tatti-taggaya-maṇo du-ssīlo aliya-jampao cavalo

jattha na dīsaï loo vaṇaṁ pi taṁ c'eva ramaṇīyaṁ.

jo jassa niya-sahāvo sajjaṇa-saṁge vi so tahā ceva

bāṇo dhamme vi ṭhio guṇa-gahio taha vi mārei.

khala-saṁge pariharie, pecchaha tilleṇa jaṁ phalaṁ pattaṁ:

mayaṇāhi-surabhi-gandhaṁ pahu-sīsaṁ pāviyaṁ jeṇa.

jehiṁ ciya ubbhaviyā jāṇa pasāeṇa vaḍḍhiya-payāvā

samarā ḍahanti viṁjhaṁ: khalāṇa saṁgo cciya viṇāso.

suyaṇāṇa neurāṇa ya suvvanto sui-maṇoraho saddo

so c'eva kaṇṇa-kaḍuo niyalāṇaṁ daḍḍha-pisuṇāṇaṁ.

nāgehĩ vaṇaṁ kāgehĩ uvavaṇaṁ rāulaṁ pisuṇaehiṁ

ruddhaṁ mayarehĩ jalaṁ: haya-vihi suyaṇo kahiṁ jāu.

taṁ natthi narenda-gharaṁ sayale mahi-maṇḍale bhamantāṇaṁ

jattha a-kāraṇa-kuviyā do tiṇṇi khalā na dīsanti.

accanta-nimmalāṇa vi molla-mahagghāṇa sāra-garuyāṇaṁ

maṇiyārŏ vva khala-yaṇo rayaṇāṇa vi kuṇaï chiḍḍāiṁ.

jāsiṁ c'eva naīṇaṁ vasahā ghuṇṭanti pāṇiyaṁ tisiyā

tāsiṁ c'eva naīṇaṁ siṁgehiṁ taḍī viṇāsenti.

sarise māṇusa-jamme, ḍahaï khalo saj-jaṇo suhāvei:

lohaṁ ciya sannāho rakkhaï jīyaṁ asī haraï.

jonhā-jaleṇa cando dhuvaï kalaṁkaṁ disā-vahu-muhāṇa

na ya dhuvaï niya-kalaṁkaṁ; savvo mujjhaï sa-kajjesu.

jo jassa vaṭṭaĕ hiyaĕ so taṁ ṭhāvei sundara-sahāvaṁ:

vagghī-chāvaṁ jaṇaṇī bhaddaṁ somaṁ ca mannei.

ratto duṭṭho mūḍho puvviṁ ku-ggāhio ya cattāri

uvaesassa aṇarihā; ariho puṇa hoi majjha-ttho.

savvo parassa pecchaï vāl'-aggāo taṇūṇi chiḍḍāṇi;

niyaya-kayāĩ na pecchaï Himagirī-sihara-ppamāṇāiṁ.

rattā pecchanti guṇā, dosā pecchanti je virajjanti;

majjha-tthā puṇa purisā dosā vi guṇā vi pecchanti.

kariṇo kari-sanghaṭṭe, patthara-niyare suvaṇṇa-reha vva

nivvaḍaï vasaṇa-kasavaṭṭayammi sāro kulīṇāṇaṁ.

sabbhāva-nehamaïe ratte raccijaï tti juttam iṇaṁ

aṇa-hiyae puṇa hiyayaṁ jaṁ dijjaï taṁ jaṇo hasaï.

hīranti je guṇehiṁ vavagaya-nehā vi je na najjanti

runnaṁ pi jesu agghaï te putta kalimmi atthamiyā.

visama-sahāvammi jaṇe na yāṇimo kaha guṇā viḍhappanti

hima-sīyale vi kiraṇe necchaï naliṇī miyankassa.

sohiya-saṁvavahāro sab-bhāvo garuyayā khamā tucchā

vihaḍanti bandhavāṇa ya kali-kāla-payāva-doseṇa.

kaṇhassa kālio phaṇa-maṇimmi siṭṭho A Ā A Ā Ā Ā

deh'-uppannā vi visaṁ cayanti deve parāhutte.

ko va na dukkhaṁ patto? suhaṁ va bhaṇa kassa sāsayaṁ atthi?

kassa nirantara-paṇao? sohaggaṁ kassa a-kkhaliyaṁ?

savvo puvva-kayāṇaṁ kammāṇaṁ pāvae phala-vivāgaṁ;

avarāhesu guṇesu ya nimitta-mettaṁ paro hoi.

nau vi karantaha su-purisaha, jaï daïvaha na suhāi

kā vi avattha sa sampaḍaï jā cintaṇaha na jāi.

annaha paricintijjaï saharisa-kaṇḍ'-ujjueṇa hiyaeṇa;

pariṇamaï annaha cciya kajjārambho vihi-vaseṇa.

vihiṇā vi naḍijjanto savvo cciya bhaṇaï aṭṭhamo cando;

candassa Rāhu-gahaṇe, taïyā ko aṭṭhamo āsi?

viupu naha-yalu rahavarāruḍhu

du-ppecchā tasu kiraṇa

attha-siharu duggamu suṇijjaï,

vihi-vihiyaṁ thū pariṇamaï

tēṇa sūru rāhĩ gilijjaï.

bhuvaṇapphālu vi dhaṇu samaï,

vabbula-heṭṭhi ṭṭhiō

ōhu pekkhu na kiṁ gilantaïṁ

dēvāha vi ēha dasa

vihi karantu kiṁ kiṁ pilijjaï?

dhannu suvaṇṇaũ ti-huyaṇahu, dinnau hōi hareṇa

tassa vi thū vihi-vihiu dhaṇu; saṅkaru naggaï teṇa.

jā taï-lukkaha nāha kira sampaï phēḍaï dēi;

taha vi jarāau Haraha dhaṇu; gali haḍḍaĩ parihēi.

annē bhaṇanti Jaṇaddaṇa Haraha tiijjau bhāu

tassa vi daïviṁ nimmiyau sayaṇiu kālau kāu.

vihi-vihiyaṁ ciya labbhaï, amayaṁ devāṇa Mahumahe Lacchī

rayaṇāyare vi mahie, Harassa bhāe visaṁ jāyaṁ.

puttaya vihi-vasi vinaḍiyaï, ko ti-ṇayaṇi sāhāru?

jiṁ Hara-vasahu vi tiṇa caraï, Vāsugi pavaṇāhāru.

jaï vi ti-ṇayaṇu ekku puhaïhiṁ

vandijjaï sura-gaṇihiṁ

tō vi tāsi vaḍha kiha ṇamijjaï?

saraṇāgau jāsu sira A

sirahu lēvi Rāhiṁ gilijjaï.

majjhe jaḍāṇa nivasantayassa katto guṇāṇa vitthāro?

Hara-sasiṇo taṁ bahuyaṁ sā vi kalā jaṁ na se jhīṇā.

thevehi vi hoi guṇehĩ gāravaṁ ni-gguṇāṇa majjhammi

bhaṇṇanti jeṇa maṇuyā, sarehĩ kūyā vi kūya-sarā.

jaha mūsagassa ekkeṇa vīhiṇā do vi vāvaḍā hatthā
