Vajjālaggaṁ

by Jayavallahō

savvannuvayaṇapaṅkayaṇivāsiṇiṁ paṇamiūṇa suyadēviṁ ।

dhammāitivaggajuyaṁ suyaṇāṇa suhāsiyaṁ vōcchaṁ ॥ 1 ॥

amayaṁ pāiyakavvaṁ paḍhiuṁ sōuṁ ca jē na jāṇanti ।

kāmassa tattavatiṁ kuṇanti tē kaha na lajjanti ॥ 2 ॥

vivihakaïviraïyāṇaṁ gāhāṇaṁ varakulāṇi ghēttūṇa ।

raïyaṁ vajjālaggaṁ vihiṇā jayavallahaṁ nāma ॥ 3 ॥

ēkkatthē patthāvē jattha paḍhijjanti paüragāhāō ।

taṁ khalu vajjālaggaṁ vajja tti ya paddhaī bhaṇiyā ॥ 4 ॥

ēyaṁ vajjālaggaṁ savvaṁ jō paḍhaï avasarammi sayā ।

pāiyakavvakaī sō hōhii taha kittimantō ya ॥ 5 ॥

dukkhaṁ kīraï kavvaṁ kavbammi kaē paüñjaṇā dukkhaṁ ।

santē paüñjamāṇē sōyārā dullahā hunti ॥ 1 ॥

sakkayamasakkayaṁ pi hu atthō sōyārasaṅgamavasēṇa ।

appuvvarasavisēsaṁ jaṇēi jaṁ taṁ mahacchariyaṁ ॥ 2 ॥

muttāhalaṁ va kavvaṁ sahāvavimalaṁ suvaṇṇasaṅghaḍiyaṁ ।

sōyārakaṇṇakuhararammi payaḍiyaṁ pāyaḍaṁ hōi ॥ 3 ॥

addhakkharabhaṇiyāiṁ nūṇaṁ savilāsamuddhahasiyāiṁ ।

addhacchipēcchiyāiṁ gāhāhi viṇā na najjanti ॥ 1 ॥

sālaṅkarāhi salakkhaṇāhi annannarāyarasiyāhiṁ ।

gāhāhi paṇaïṇīhi ya khijjaï cittaṁ aïntīhiṁ ॥ 2 ॥

ēyaṁ ciya navari phuḍaṁ hiyayaṁ gāhāṇa mahiliyāṇaṁ ca ।

aṇarasiēhi na labbhaï daviṇaṁ va vihīṇapuṇṇēhiṁ ॥ 3 ॥

sacchandiyā sarūvā sālaṅkārā ya sarasa--ullāvā ।

varakāmiṇi vva gāhā gāhijjantī rasaṁ dēi ॥ 4 ॥

gāhāṇa rasā mahilāṇa vibbhamā kaïjaṇāṇa ullāvā ।

kassaṁ na haranti hiyayaṁ bālāṇa ya mammaṇullāvā ॥ 5 ॥

savvō gāhāu jaṇō vīsatthō bhaṇaï savvagōṭhṭhīsu ।

paramatthō jō tāṇaṁ sō nāō mahachaïllēhiṁ ॥ 6 ॥

gāhā ruaï varāī sikkijjantī gavāralōēhiṁ ।

kīraï luñcapaluñcā jaha gāī mandadōhēhiṁ ॥ 7 ॥

gāhē bhajjihisi tumaṁ ahavā lahuyattaṇaṁ vi pāvihisi ।

gāmāradantadiḍhakaḍhiṇapīḍiyā ucchulaṭṭhi vva ॥ 8 ॥

gāhāṇaṁ gīyāṇaṁ tantīsaddāṇa pōḍhamahilāṇaṁ ।

tāṇaṁ ciya sō daṇḍō jē tāṇa rasaṁ na yāṇanti ॥ 9 ॥

chandaṁ ayāṇa māṇēhi jā kiyā sā na hōi ramaṇijjā ।

kiṁ gāhā aha sēvā ahavā gāhā vi sēvā vi ॥ 10 ॥

cintāmandaramanthāṇamanthiē vittharammi atthāhē ।

uppajjanti kaīhiyayasāyarē kavvarayaṇāiṁ ॥ 1 ॥

rayaṇujjalapayasōhaṁ taṁ kavvaṁ jaṁ tavēi paḍivakkhaṁ ।

purisāyantavilāsiṇirasaṇādāmaṁ miva rasantaṁ ॥ 2 ॥

pāiyakavvammi rasō jō jāyaï taha ya chēyamaṇiēhiṁ ।

uyayasya ya vāsiyasīyalassa tittiṁ na vaccāmō ॥ 3 ॥

kaha kaha vi raēi payaṁ maggaṁ pulaēi chēyamāruhaï ।

cōrō vva kaī atthaṁ ghēttūṇaṁ kaha vi nivvahaï ॥ 4 ॥

saddāvasaddabhīrū paē paē kiṁ pi kiṁ pi cintantō ।

dukkhōhi kaha vi pāvaï cōrō atthaṁ kaī kavvaṁ ॥ 5 ॥

saddapalōṭṭaṁ dōsēhi vajjiyaṁ sulaliyaṁ phuḍaṁ mahuraṁ

puṇṇēhi kaha vi pāvaï chandē kavvaṁ kalattaṁ ca ॥ 6 ॥

aṇavarayabahalarōmañcakañcuyaṁ jaṇiyajaṇamaṇāṇandaṁ ।

jaṁ na dhuṇāvaï sīsaṁ kavvaṁ pēmmaṁ ca kiṁ tēṇa ॥ 7 ॥

sō sōhaï dūsantō kaïyaṇaraïyāi vivihakavvāiṁ ।

jō bhañjiūṇa avayaṁ1 annapayaṁ sundaraṁ dēi ॥ 8 ॥

atthakkō rasarahiō dēsavihīṇō'ṇuṇāsiō turiō ।

muhavañcaṇō virāō ēē dōsā paḍhantassa ॥ 9 ॥

dēsiyasaddapalōdr̥ṁ mahurakkharachandasaṇṭhiyaṁ laliyaṁ ।

phuḍaviyaḍapāyaḍatthaṁ pāiyakavvaṁ paḍhēyavvaṁ ॥ 10 ॥

laliē mahurakkharaē juvaījaṇavallahē sasiṅgārē ।

santē pāiyakavvē kō sakkaï sakkayaṁ paḍhiuṁ ॥ 11 ॥

abuhā buhāṇa majjhē paḍhanti jē chandalakkhaṇavihūṇā ।

tē bhamuhākhaggaṇivāḍiyaṁ pi sīsaṁ na lakkhanti ॥ 12 ॥

pāiyakavvassa namō pāiyakavvaṁ ca nimmiyaṁ jēṇa ।

tāhaṁ ciya paṇamāmō paḍhiūṇa ya jē vi thāṇanti ॥13 ॥

mahaṇammi sasī mahaṇammi suratarū mahaṇasambhavā lacchī ।

suyaṇō uṇa kahasu mahaṁ na yāṇimō kattha sambhūō ॥ 1 ॥

suyaṇō suddhasahāvō maïlijjantō vi dujjaṇajaṇēṇa ।

chārēṇa dappaṇō viya ahiyayaraṁ nimmalō hōi ॥ 2 ॥

suyaṇō na kuppaï cciya aha kuppaï maṅgulaṁ na cintēi ।

aha cintēi na jampaï aha jampaï lajjirō hōi ॥ 3 ॥

daḍharōsakalusiyassa vi suyaṇassa suhāu vippiyaṁ kattō ।

rāhumuhammi vi sasiṇō kiraṇā amayaṁ ciya muyanti ॥ 4 ॥

diṭṭhā haranti dukkhaṁ jampantā dēnti sayalasōkkhāiṁ ।

ēyaṁ vihiṇā sukayaṁ suyaṇā jaṁ nimmiyā bhuvaṇē ॥ 5 ॥

na hasanti paraṁ na thuvanti appayaṁ piyasayāi jampanti ।

ēsō suyaṇasahāvō namō namō tāṇa purisāṇaṁ ॥ 6 ॥

akaē vi kaē vi piē piyaṁ kuṇantā jayammi dīsanti ।

kayavippiē vi hu piyaṁ kuṇanti tē dullahā suyaṇā ॥ 7 ॥

savvassa ēha payaī piyammi uppāiē piyaṁ kāuṁ ।

suyaṇassa ēha payaī akaē vi piē piyaṁ kāuṁ ॥ 8 ॥

pharusaṁ na bhaṇasi bhaṇiō vi hasasi hasiūṇa jampasi piyāiṁ ।

sajjaṇa tujjha sahāvō na yāṇimō kassa sāricchō ॥ 9 ॥

nēcchasi parāvayāraṁ parōvayāraṁ ca niccamāvahasi ।

avarāhēhi1 na kuppasi suyaṇa namō tuha sahāvassa ॥ 10 ॥

dōhiṁ ciya pajjattaṁ bahuēhi vi kiṁ guṇōhi suyaṇassa ।

vijjupphuriyaṁ2 rōsō mittī pāhāṇarēha vva ॥ 11 ॥

rē rē kalikālamahāgaïnda galagajjiyassa kō kālō ।

ajja vi supurisakēsarikisōracalaṇaṅkiyā puhavī ॥ 12 ॥

dīṇaṁ abbhuddhariuṁ pattē saraṇāgaē piyaṁ kāuṁ ।

avaraddhēsu vi khamiuṁ suyaṇō cciya navari jāṇēi ॥ 13 ॥

vē purisā dharaï dharā ahavā dōhiṁ pi dhāriyā dharaṇī ।

uvayārē jassa maī uvayariyaṁ jō na pamhusaï ॥ 14 ॥

paḍivajjanti na suyaṇā aha paḍivajjanti kaha vi dukkhēhiṁ ।

patthararēha vva samā maraṇō vi na annahā hōi ॥ 15 ॥

sēlā calanti palaē majjāyaṁ sāyarā vi mēllanti ।

suyaṇā tahiṁ pi kālē paḍivannaṁ nēya siḍhilanti ॥ 16 ॥

candaṇataru vva suyaṇā phalarahiyā jaï vi nimmiyā vihiṇā ।

taha vi kuṇanti paratthaṁ niyayasarīrēṇa lōyassa ॥ 17 ॥

hayadujjaṇassa vayaṇaṁ nirantaraṁ bahalakajjalacchāyaṁ ।

saṅkuddhaṁ bhiuḍijuyaṁ kayā vi na hu nimmalaṁ diṭṭhaṁ ॥ 1 ॥

thaddhō vaṅkaggīvō avañciō visamadiṭṭhiduppēcchō ।

ahiṇavariddhi vva khalō sūlādinnu1 vva paḍihāi ॥ 2 ॥

nahamāsabhēyajaṇaṇō dummuhaō atthikhaṇḍaṇasamatthō ।

taha vi hu majjhāvaliō namaha khalō naharaṇasaricchō ॥ 3 ॥

akulīṇō dōmuhaō tā mahurō bhōyaṇaṁ muhē jāva ।

muraü vva khalō jiṇṇāmmi bhōyaṇē virasamārasaï ॥ 4 ॥

niddhammō guṇarahiō ṭhāṇavimukkō ya lōhasambhūō ।

vindhaï jaṇassa hiyayaṁ pisuṇō bāṇu vva laggantō ॥ 5 ॥

jammē vi jaṁ na hūyaṁ na hu hōsaï jaṁ ca jammala kkhē vi ।

taṁ jampanti taha cciya pisuṇā jaha hōi sāricchaṁ ॥ 6 ॥

guṇiṇō guṇēhi vihavēhi vihaviṇō hōntu gavviyā nāma ।

dōsēhi navari gavvō khalāṇa maggō cciya aüvvō ॥ 7 ॥

santaṁ na dēnti vārēnti dēntayaṁ dinnayaṁ pi hāranti ।

aṇimittavaïriyāṇaṁ khalāṇa maggō cciya aüvvō ॥ 8 ॥

paravivaraladdhalakkhē cittalaē bhīsaṇē jamalajīhē ।

vaṅkaparisakkirē gōṇasē vva pisuṇē suhaṁ kattō ॥ 9 ॥

asamatthamantatantāṇa kulavimukkāṇa mōyahīṇāṇaṁ ।

diṭṭhāṇa kō na bīhaï vintarasappāṇa va khalāṇaṁ ॥ 10 ॥

ēyaṁ ciya bahulāhō jīvijjaï jaṁ khalāṇa majjhammi ।

lāhō jaṁ na ḍasijjaï bhuyaṅgaparivēḍhiē calaṇē ॥ 11 ॥

na sahaï abbhatthaṇiyaṁ asaï gayāṇaṁ pi piṭṭhimaṁsāiṁ ।

daṭṭhūṇa bhāsuramuhaṁ khalasīhaṁ kō na bīhēi ॥ 12 ॥

mā vaccaï vīsambhaṁ pamuhē bahukūḍakavaḍabhariyāṇaṁ ।

nivvattiya kajjaparaṁmuhāṇa suṇayāṇa va khalāṇaṁ ॥ 13 ॥

jēhiṁ ciya ubbhaviyā jāṇa pasāēṇa niggayapayāvā ।

samarā ḍahanti viñjhaṁ khalāṇa maggō cciya aüvvō ॥ 14 ॥

sarasā vi dumā dāvāṇalēṇa ḍajjhanti sukkhasaṁvaliyā ।

dujjaṇasaṅgē pattē suyaṇō vi suhaṁ na pāvēi ॥ 15 ॥

khalasajjaṇāṇa dōsā guṇā ya kō vaṇṇiuṁ taraï lōē ।

jaï navari nāyarāō dōhiṁ jīhāsahassēhiṁ ॥ 16 ॥

ēkkaṁ ciya salahijjaï diṇēsadiyahāṇa navari nivvahaṇaṁ ।

ājamma ēkkamēkkēhi jēhi virahō cciya na diṭṭhō ॥ 1 ॥

paḍivannaṁ diṇayaravāsarāṇa dōṇhaṁ akhaṇḍiyaṁ suhaï ।

sūrō na diṇēṇa viṇā diṇō vi na hu suravirahammi ॥ 2 ॥

mittaṁ payatōyasamaṁ sāricchaṁ jaṁ na hōi kiṁ tēṇa ।

ahiyāēi milantaṁ āvaï āvaṭṭaē paḍhamaṁ ॥ 3 ॥

taṁ mittaṁ kāyavvaṁ jaṁ kira vasaṇammi dēsakālammi ।

ālihiyamittibāullayaṁ va na paraṁmuhaṁ ṭhāi ॥ 4 ॥

taṁ mittaṁ kāyavvaṁ jaṁ mittaṁ kālakambalīsarisaṁ ।

uyaēṇa dhōyamāṇaṁ sahāvaraṅgaṁ na mēllēi ॥ 5 ॥

saguṇāṇa nigguṇāṇa ya garuyā pālanti jaṁ ji paḍivannaṁ ।

pēcchaha vasahēṇa samaṁ harēṇa vōlāviō appā ॥ 6 ॥

chijjaü sīsaṁ aha hōu bandhaṇaṁ cayaü savvahā lacchī ।

paḍivannapālaṇē supurisāṇa jaṁ hōi taṁ hōu ॥ 7 ॥

diḍhalōhasaṅkalāṇaṁ annāṇa vi vivihapāsabandhāṇaṁ ।

tāṇaṁ ciya ahiyayaraṁ vāyābandhaṁ kulīṇassa ॥ 8 ॥

candō dhavalijjaï puṇṇimāi aha puṇṇimā vi candēṇa ।

samasuhadukkhāi maṇē puṇṇēṇa viṇā na labbhanti ॥ 1 ॥

ēkkāi navari nēhō payāsiō tihuyaṇammi jōṇhāē ।

jā jhijjaï jhīṇē sasaharammi vaḍḍhēi vaḍḍhantē ॥ 2 ॥

jhijjaï jhīṇammi sayā vaḍḍhaï vaḍḍhantayammi savisēsaṁ ।

sāyarasasīṇa chajjaï jayammi paḍivannaṇivvahaṇaṁ ॥ 3 ॥

paḍivannaṁ jēṇa samaṁ puvvaṇiōēṇa hōi jīvassa ।

dūraṭiṭhaō na dūrē jaha candō kumuyasaṇḍāṇaṁ ॥ 4 ॥

dūraṭiṭhayā na dūrē sajjaṇacittāṇa puvvamiliyāṇaṁ ।

gayaṇaṭriṭhaō vi candō āsāsaï kumuyasaṇḍāiṁ ॥ 5 ॥

diṭṭhē vi hu hōi suhaṁ jaï vi na pāvanti aṅgasaṅgāiṁ ।

dūraṭṭhiō vi candō suṇivvuiṁ kuṇaï kumuyāṇaṁ ॥ 6 ॥

ēmēva kaha vi kassa vi kēṇa vi diṭṭhēṇa hōi pariōsō ।

kamalāyarāṇa raïṇā kiṁ kajjaṁ jēṇa viyasanti ॥ 7 ॥

kattō uggamaï raī kattō viyasanti paṅkayavaṇāiṁ ।

suyaṇāṇa jaē nēhō na calaï dūraṭṭhiyāṇaṁ pi ॥ 8 ॥

jaṁ jassa mammamēyaṁ cālijjantaṁ ca dūmaē hiyayaṁ ।

taṁ tassa kaṇṇakaḍuyaṁ kulēsu jāyā na japāta ॥ 1 ॥

santēha asantēhi ya parassa kiṁ jampiēhi dōsēhiṁ ॥

atthō jasō na labbhaï sō vi amittō kaō hōi ॥ 2 ॥

appahiyaṁ kāyavvaṁ jaï sakkaï parahiyaṁ ca kāyavvaṁ ।

appahiyaparahiyāṇaṁ appahiyaṁ cēva kāyavvaṁ ॥ 3 ॥

purisē saccasamiddhē aliyapamukkē sahāvasantuṭṭhē ।

tavadhammaṇiyamamaïē visamā vi dasā samā hōi ॥ 4 ॥

sīlaṁ varaṁ kulāō dāliddaṁ bhavvayaṁ ca rōgāō ।

vijjā rajjāu varaṁ khamā varaṁ suṭṭhu vi tavāō ॥ 5 ॥

sīlaṁ varaṁ kulāō kulēṇa kiṁ hōi vigayasīlēṇa ।

kamalāi kaddamē sambhavanti na hu hunti maliṇāiṁ ॥ 6 ॥

jaṁ ji khamēi samatthō ghaṇavantō jaṁ na gavvamuvvahaï ।

jaṁ ca savijjō namirō tisu tēsu alaṅkiyā puhabī ॥ 7 ॥

chandaṁ jō aṇuvaṭṭaï mammaṁ rakkhaï guṇē payāsēi ।

sō navari māṇusāṇaṁ dēvāṇa vi vallahō hōi ॥ 8 ॥

chaṇavañcaṇēṇa varisō nāsaï divasō kubhōyaṇē bhuttē ।

kukalattēṇa ya jammō nāsaï dhammō ahammēṇa ॥ 9 ॥

channaṁ dhammaṁ payaḍaṁ ca pōrisaṁ parakalattavañcaṇayaṁ ।

gañjaṇarahiō jammō rāḍhāittāṇa sampaḍaï ॥ 10 ॥

appāṇaṁ amuyantā jē ārambhanti duggamaṁ kajjaṁ ।

paramuhapalōiyāṇaṁ tāṇaṁ kaha hōi jayalacchī ॥ 1 ॥

sigdhaṁ āruha kajjaṁ pāraddhaṁ mā kahaṁ pi siḍhilēsu ।

pāraddhasiḍhiliyāiṁ kajjāi puṇō na sijjhanti ॥ 2 ॥

acchaü tā iyarajaṇō aṅgē cciya jāi pañca bhūyāiṁ ।

tāhaṁ ciya lajjijjaï pāraddhaṁ pariharantēṇa ॥ 3 ॥

jhīṇavihavō vi suyaṇō sēvaï rannaṁ na patthaē annaṁ ।

maraṇē vi aïmahagdhaṁ na vikkiṇaï māṇamāṇikkaṁ ॥ 4 ॥

vē maggā bhuvaṇayalē māṇiṇi māṇunnayāṇa purisāṇaṁ ।

ahavā pāvanti siriṁ ahava bhamantā samappanti ॥ 5 ॥

bēṇṇi vi hunti gaīō sāhasavantāṇa dhīrapurisāṇaṁ ।

vēllahalakamalahatthā rāyasirī ahava pavvajjā ॥ 6 ॥

ahavā maranti guruvasaṇapēlliyā khaṇḍiūṇa niyajīhaṁ ।

nō gantūṇa khalāṇaṁ cavanti dīṇakkharaṁ dhīrā ॥ 7 ॥

aha suppaï piyamāliṅgiūṇa uttuṅgathōrathaṇavaṭṭhē ।

aha narakaraṅkakaṅkālasaṅkulē bhīsaṇamasāṇē ॥ 8 ॥

aha bhuñjaï saha piyakāmiṇīhi kaccōlathālasippīhiṁ ।

ahavā vimalakavālē bhikkhaṁ bhamiūṇa pēyavaṇē ॥ 9 ॥

namiūṇa jaṁ viḍhappaï khalacalaṇaṁ tihuyaṇaṁ pi kiṁ tēṇa ।

māṇēṇa jaṁ viḍhappaï taṇaṁ pi taṁ nivvuiṁ kuṇaï ॥ 10 ॥

tē dhannā tāṇa namō tē garuyā māṇiṇō thirārambhā ।

jē garuyavasaṇapaḍipēlliyā vi annaṁ na patthanti ॥ 11 ॥

tuṅgō cciya hōi maṇō maṇaṁsiṇō antimāsu vi dasāsu ।

atthantassa vi raïṇō kiraṇā uddhaṁ ciya phuranti ॥ 12 ॥

tā tuṅgō mērugirī mayaraharō tāva hōi duttārō ।

tā visamā kajjagaī jāva na dhīrā pavajjanti ॥ 13 ॥

tā vitthiṇṇaṁ gayaṇaṁ tāva cciya jalaharā aïgahīrā ।

tā garuyā kulasēlā jāva na dhīrēhi tullanti ॥ 14 ॥

mērū tiṇaṁ va saggō gharaṅgaṇaṁ hatthachittaṁ gayaṇayalaṁ ।

vāhaliyā ya samuddā sāhasavantāṇa purisāṇaṁ ॥ 15 ॥

saṅghaḍiyaghaḍiyavighaḍiyaghaḍantavighaḍantasaṅghaḍijjantaṁ ।

avahatthiūṇa divvaṁ karēi dhīrō samāraddhaṁ ॥ 16 ॥

sāhasamavalambantō pāvaï hiyaïcchiyaṁ na sandēhō ।

jēṇuttamaṅgamēttēṇa rāhuṇā kavaliō candō ॥ 1 ॥

taṁ kiṁ pi sāhasaṁ sāhasēṇa sāhanti sāhasasahāvā ।

jaṁ bhāviūṇa divvō paraṁmuhō dhuṇaï niyasīsaṁ ॥ 2 ॥

tharatharaï dharā khubbhanti sāyarā hōi vimhalō1 daïvō ।

asamavavasāyasāhasasaṁladdhajasāṇa dhīrāṇaṁ ॥ 3 ॥
