Subhāsiyapajjasaṅgahō

by Unknown Author

kiṁ bhaṇimō navajuvaīṇa tāṇa savvaṅgacaṅgimasuhassa

diṭṭhītibhāyaphaṁsē vi jāṇamamayaṁ pi hu na kipi

gayanēvacchā vacchē! aïdusahā taṁ si taruṇanayaṇāṇa

vammahakarasaṅgahiyā nippaḍiyāra vva khaggalayā

diṭṭhīē tassa sasaharakarasalilajjhalajhalāsalōṇāē

vālā sasimaṇidhīulliya vva sēulliyā jāyā

navasaṅgaraṅganaṭṭāvaēṇa naccāviyā tahā ahayaṁ

māmi! mahaṁ ciya hiyayaṁ na vimhayaṁ jēṇa pamhusaï

amayamaō vi hu dāhaṁ jaṇēi, sañjīvaṇō vi mōhēi

maüō vi vindhaï maṇaṁ, diṭṭhicchōhō mayacchīṇaṁ

aïvaṅkadīharā jaṇamaṇāiṁ mōhanti kulavahukaḍakkhā

aṇivāriya aṇivarisiya visamavisā rōsāruṇakasiṇabhuyaga vva

annēsiṁ sōhaggaṁ na sahanti, na dinti kahavi ya suhacchiṁ

jīyaṁ haranti virahē ahō! guṇā dujjaṇā tīē

kuppanti vallahatthē apahuppantassa niyayaaṅgassa

annapahuppantassa ya puṇō asūyanti mihuṇāiṁ

aṭṭhāṇaṭṭhanilayaṇaṁ visamiyasayaṇaṁ khalantamiuvayaṇaṁ

vārattanimiyanayaṇaṁ pacchannarayaṁ kahaṁ suhayaṁ?

vallahajaṇassa dukkhē nisuē virahammi jāyaē sukkhaṁ

sukkhammi puṇō dukkhaṁ pimmassa gaī ahō! visamā

jassantarāvayāsō gaüravadakkhinnadujjaṇajaṇāṇa

taṁ pi hu pemmaṁ ti bhaṇantayāṇa jīhā vi lajjēi

taṁ suyaṇā jaṁ vasiyā si takkhaṇa ccēya tassa hiyayammi

sō dujjaṇō uṇa na jō ajja vi nivasēi tuha hiyaē

maṁ pariciyaṁ pi ujjhiya dullahalambhē jaṇammi saṅkanta!

hā! hiyaya! hīṇasāhasa! lahasi tumaṁ dukkhalakkhāiṁ

vallahasayavāsayamandirēsu sañjhabbhasarisarāēsu

purisahiyaēsu pemmāvandhō kō nāma vāmanayaṇāṇaṁ?

avarāhō vi hu vallahajaṇassa paramaṁ pamōyamappēi

ahiyaṁ harēi hiyaya dhūmō vi hu surahivatthūṇaṁ

jaggirasōhaggāṇaṁ aviṇayavāṇī vi varisaē harisaṁ

suhaḍanivakāyarō vi hu kiṁ na hu jayamajjaï jayammi?

sō atthō jō hatthē, taṁ mittaṁ jaṁ nirantaraṁ vasaṇē

taṁ rūvaṁ jattha guṇā, ta vinnāṇaṁ jahiṁ dhammō

sō suhaō parama ppuvvilla ppuvvalira nihāṇaṁ va naṭṭhasallaṁ va

āsannō vi hu vihiō hayavihi! kaha nāma maha dūrē?

dūraṭṭhiō vi su cciya maha hiyaē jalaharō vva sarasīē

sē vva ṇa naliṇīva ṇavaṇa ? gaēhi kiṁ nāma annēhiṁ?

kuviēṇa kayantēṇaṁ parivārē vāriyammi nisi vi samayē

kulabāliyāē lajjā mahalliyā sahayarī hōi

aïmuddhamaüyacavalō valantanayaṇāi tīē jantīē

diṭṭhicchōhō savisēsadūsahō vālabhuyagō vva

saccaṁ raī ciya imā jamimīi mamaṁ

paharēi sō hayāsō visamēhiṁ sarēhi kusumasarō

kōdaṇḍakōsalēṇaṁ sō ajja vi lajjiro cciya aṇaṅgō

jaṁ kira lakkhaṁ bhuyaṇāiṁ tinni, bāṇā puṇō pañca

amayarasasāramullañciūṇa candāō tuha muhaṁ vihiyaṁ

tassa talaṁ jēṇa jaṇō suyaṇu! kalaṅko tti kittēi

āyannāṇa guṇāṇaṁ raṇaraṇaō, sumaraṇammi dāhajarō

diṭṭhē hallapphalaō, miliyammi piyammi kimpi suhaṁ

diṭṭhaṁ siṇiddhanayaṇaṁ vayaṇaṁ viṇaōvarōhasōhillaṁ

karakamalakēsabandhō ya haraï dhīraṁ hiyāhintō

ēsō aladdhadullahakāmō vāmēyarō karō phuriō

dāhiṇanayaṇakayatthassa tujjha kaha nāma vāmattaṁ?

rūvaṁ nayaṇāṇa guṇā maṇassa avalōyaṇāiṁ caraṇāṇaṁ

ālavaṇaṁ savvaṅgassa bandhaṇaṁ tassa kiṁ bhaṇimō?

‘sumarijjasu’ tti pimmassa bāhiraṁ, ‘maha suhē’ti na ghaḍēi

‘mā khijjasu’ tti aṇuhavaviruddhamiha kiṁ bhaṇāmi ahaṁ?

nayaṇāṇa vandhaṇaṁ maṇaviāraṇaṁ vēyaṇāi sammōhō

annāṇa dasaṇijjāṇa dujjaṇō tīya rūvasirī

amayamayavayaṇamavirāmaramiyamaviyāravajjariyakajjaṁ

jayaï pahuttaṁ vālattaṇaṁ va pemma ca lōyammi

gaṅgāsaṅgēṇa viṇā vi jalanihī hōi jalanihi sa vva yā

gaṅgā uṇa tēṇa viṇā bhaṇa kattha khaṇaṁ pi vīsamaü?

ghariṇī lacchi vva mahānihi vva amayaṁ va punnamutti vva

jattha'tthi gharaṁ ta ciya, annaṁ rannaṁ ti mannanti

tāō guṇāṇa khāṇī mahimaḍalamaṇḍaṇaṁ sumahilāō

dōsā vi jattha nihiyā guṇarūvēṇaṁ pariṇamanti

tāṇa kulavāliyāṇaṁ na lahaï saccaṁ sahāvamahimāṇaṁ

jāṇa paḍivakkhacaḍiyā dōsā vi maṇaṁ na dūminti

tāṇa namō samaṇīṇaṁ ājammaṁ cariyavambhacērāṇaṁ

pimmanaḍakavaḍanāḍayaviḍaṁvaṇā jāṇa na khaṇaṁ pi

tīē tijayaṁ pi kīraü niuñchaṇaṁ picchulucchujaṭṭhīē

chittā vi pīliyā vi hu jā na hu mahurattaṇaṁ muaï

mahilā pasāyagahilā sayampabhā hōi, na uṇa sappurisō

sasiṇā viṇā vi rayaṇī havaï, na divasō viṇā raviṇā

mannāmi māmi! ki vā majjha kaē tujjha dukkaraṁ annaṁ?

suhayassa majjha tassa jaṁ kira appā vi samappiō tumaē

taṁ dacchanti saharisaṁ kayāvarāhaṁ ahaṁ ti savilakkhaṁ

puṇaravi caraṇaṁ disa rōsa māmi! nalamāṇasaṁ taïyā ?

taṇhāē ? taralanayaṇō parissamē, añcalammi valahatthō

dukkhē sattō, dhammammi vimhiō, tuha suhē suhiō

jūyaṁ dhaṇāṇa, vasaṇaṁ guṇāṇa, paḍipatthaṇōvayārāṇa

vatthuviyārō pimmāṇa māṇamummūlaï samūlaṁ

lajjā kulamajjāyā dunni kavāḍāiṁ jēhiṁ bhaggāiṁ

hiyaēsu tēsu sahasa tti dukkhamakkhaliyamalliyaï

pimmassa pōḍhamahilājaṇassa vihavassa vārivāhassa

paramappaṇō maṇassa ya muṇanti muṇiṇō viṇayarūvaṁ

titthāṇa supurisāṇaṁ maṇīṇa santāṇamōsahīṇaṁ ca

mahimā mahāsaīṇaṁ ca kālakalusassa dūrēṇaṁ

nayaraṁ mahūsavamayaṁ, tucchā lacchī, maṇaṁ mahārambhaṁ

vihinihiyaṁ maha hiyaē tisallayaṁ sallaē ēyaṁ

vittavivāhaṁ ca gihaṁ, payapabbhaṭṭhō ya naravaïniōī

gayajovvaṇā ya mahilā na puvvasōhaṁ pi hu lahanti

vihavaṅgaṇāṇa gabbhō, niddhaṇavaṅgāṇa dāṇavasaṇāiṁ

ja ca khavaṇāṇa kuccaṁ taṁ amayachalaṁ halāhalayaṁ

aïkiviṇō cēya tumaṁ jaṁ ikkammi vi guṇammi ghippantē

apparimāṇaguṇō vi hu vahasi muhamahōmuhaṁ nāha!

na hu tuha guṇē gahissaṁ ? duṇha pi jāṇa gahaṇammi

tuha gahaṇāsahaṇēṇaṁ maha uṇa aïmucchagahaṇēṇaṁ

samaratimirammi sahalaṁ dūyaṁ piva pēsiūṇa pattavahaṁ

ahisāraï riulacchiṁ mahalliyā jassa cāvalayā

taïṁ giṇhiya cāvalaē riūṇa lacchī ti gahiyacāvalayā

rattā tumammi naravaï! darisaï sarisattaṇaṁ nūṇaṁ

unnamiūṇa nahayalē jalahara! tuha gajjiya pi chajjēi

vajjēṇa dahiumamaēṇa siñciuṁ vayasi ja bhuyaṇaṁ

jaï vihavīṇa viṇīyaṁ diṭṭhipahē kahavi vaccaï avaccaṁ

tā amaya-canda-candima-candaṇapaṅkēhiṁ na hu kipi

mannanti duhaṁ pi suhaṁ mahilāō avaccanēhagahilāō

parabālalālaṇaṁ pi hu ja kira vajhāō jhāyati

dhannāō tāō kulavāliyāō nimmahiyamōhamahimāō

diṭṭha na viyaḍabhiuḍīpuḍakiyaṁ jāhi paravayaṇa

muttu mālaïpamuhaṁ kusumasamūhaṁ mahūsavadiṇēsu

ekka ciya gijjaï bāliyāhiṁ nōmāliyākusumaṁ

suhaḍanarindapayāvō sumantajāvō pasūṇasaracāvō

taha ya mahāsaïsāvō mā kayamavvō! niyattēi

niccaṁ varisasu dāṇaṁ kuṇēsu gamaṇaṁ maṇōharagaīē

daṁsasu dasaṇē cavalē! jaï si suhatthī-maṁ bhaddē!

vērīṇaṁ maraṇabhayaṁ, bandhūṇa maṇōrahō, suhaṁ haliṇō

gōvīṇa vallaharasō, kaṇhēṇa sama vivaḍḍhanti

pīūsappamuhāṇa vi suyaṇō dippēvi dēi dōhaggaṁ

ru ṭṭhō ddhō vi dujjaṇō uṇa dahaṇāīṇaṁ pi sōhaggaṁ

mahimaṁ mahāsaīṇaṁ kiṁ bhaṇimō jāṇa pāṇimāhappā

ārambhō mokkhaphalō jāyaï, lacchī vi kāmaduhā

uvajujjanti kayā vi hu na hi jē pattēsu kaha vi pattēsu

caṇḍālakūvajalasacchahēhi kiṁ tēhi vihavēhiṁ?

vihaliyasāhārattaṁ ki bhaṇimō tāṇa dujjaṇajaṇāṇaṁ

duhiya-suhiēhiṁ mukkē suha-dukkhē jēsu allīṇē

dāṇaṁ dīṇuddharaṇaṁ āgamasavaṇaṁ sutitthajattā ya

pavayaṇabhattī a maṇussajammataruṇō phalasamiddhī

kiṁ tēṇāvi ghaḍijjaï jō na hu sabbhāvarasanisittō vi

purisō ya puhaïpiṇḍō va bhijjiō appamappēi

viyaliyadhaṇāṇa dāṇaṁ, jarajajjarajuvvaṇāṇa pariṇayaṇaṁ

maraṇasaraṇāṇa dhammō, kisikammaṁ sarayasamayammi

aviyali ya mappaṇu cciya sayalāō suhaṁ mahanti mahilāō

niyapaïṇo cciya suhamahilasanti jā tāō puṇa viralā

jē varisantē vi ciraṁ pukkhalamēhē gurussa amiyarasaṁ

bhijjanti kimpi na hu muggasēlasīlāṇa tāṇa namō

jaha sajalamēhalēhāē pāusō, māhavīē mahusamaō

taha lahaï siriṁ purisō guṇavaṇadharaṇīē ghariṇīē

pimmaṁ ta ciya jāyanti jattha dōsā vi naṇu guṇa ccēya

siddharasō sō kira jattha hunti lōhāiṁ vi suvannaṁ

tē kē vi kamaliṇīē vi havanti santāvadīharā diyahā

mittō vi jēsu saha jīvaṇēṇa jīyaṁ pi saṁharaï

supurisabhariyāē vi ni ṭṭhi hi yāē vambhaṇḍabhaṇḍasālāē

sampaï purisā kamasō avayaramiyaru vva uvvariyā ?

virahēṇaṁ ciya piyasaṅgasāṇa sōhaggamahiyamullasaï

gimhēṇaṁ ciya sīyalajalāṇa mahamahaï māhappaṁ

dappō guṇāṇa, niddā suyāṇa, vasaṇaṁ gurūvaēsāṇa

paṇasundarī dhaṇāṇaṁ, lōhō sukkhāṇa khayavāhī

pūnantā dēsantarēhi suhu sāmuhaüṁ hasēi

punnahīṇu puṇu niyagharaha bhittari bhuvakha bhamēi

baddhā gāḍhaṁ, gūḍhaṁ ca dhāriyā, nihaṇiyā ya khaḍḍāē

khaṇḍīkayā ya kiviṇēṇa kaha va na vi mōciyā lacchī

īsā-visāya-bhaya-kōha- lōha- annāṇa-māṇa-mayaṇēhiṁ

sasāranāḍayanaḍā naccāvijjanti jantugaṇā

annāyaguṇavisēsammi sāmiē kiṁ kuṇanti guṇavantā?

putti! na muttiyamagghaï guñjāharaṇē pulindammi

kiṁ dullahamuvaēsō, kiṁ dūrē? jaṁ maṇassa uvviddhaṁ

kiṁ rammaṁ? jattha raī, kimuvvasaṁ? jattha na hu suyaṇō

gāṇaṁ kaṇṭhapamāṇaṁ, māṇaṁ pimmāṇumāṇamiha suhayaṁ

jāṇaṁ āsaṇanāṇaṁ ? , dāṇaṁ niyavibhavaparimāṇaṁ

tāva hu sīlaṁ lajjā gurūvaēsā vasanti hiyayammi

lōyassa ē ṇa jaggaï jaṭharaggipalīvaṇaṁ jāva

sasiṇēhadēhadahaṇē kiṁ bhaṇimō tassa virahadahaṇassa?

jassa paḍiyārakaraṇē vi ānalō candaṇarasō vva

candamuhi! amayavayaṇē! nīluppalanayaṇi! rambhakhambhōru!

virahavihiyammi hiyaē nihiyā vi hu kaha ṇu taṁ tavasi?

viṇaēhi guṇēhintō vi nūṇa taṁ cēva sasimuhi! mahagghā

jaṁ tē vi taē gahiyā amayaṁ visaṁ va puṇṇarahiyā ?

ahaha! maha maṇuyajammō musiō mūḍhassa visayacōrēhiṁ

andhassa va vaṇiṇō khajjamāvaṇā ḍimbharūēhiṁ

jā kuḍilanīyagamaṇā taṁ jālayaṁ pi muttumōsaraï ?

sā tumaē maïparihīṇa! mīṇa! sariyā kahaṁ mariyā ?

vēhavvaniccadukkhaṁ va vīyaghariṇittaṇaṁ va dahiūṇa

sampaï païmmi vihiē vahāmi sōyaṁ pamōyaṁ vā

īsarasiripattō vi hu tuha mahaṇijjō vi pa a mayamottī vi

punnattaṇaṁ na pāvaï sasī jaḍāsaṅgadōsēṇa

ahaha! vihiṇō paüttaṁ jaṁ sayamavi nimmiyammi kammammi

ciṭṭhantu samōvāyā vallaha dulahaṁ paridēviyavvammi

jē nivvāsaviyā vi hu khaṇanti dūrē kayā dahanti jaṇaṁ

tēsu visaēsu rāhava! nāha! vaṇaṁ jujjaï buhāṇaṁ
