Dhuttakkhāṇaṁ

by Haribhaddō

namiūṇa jiṇa-var’indē tiyasēsara-vandiē dhuya-kilēsē

viusajaṇa-bōhaṇatthaṁ dhuttakkhāṇaṁ pavakkhāmi

laliya-samiddha-jaṇavayā atthi purī surapuriṁ visēsantī

ujjuya-jaṇāhivāsā ujjēṇī nāma nāmēṇaṁ

tīsē uttarapāsē nāṇāviha-kusuma-gumma-laya-gahaṇaṁ

mahuyari-gaṇōvagīyaṁ ujjāṇaṁ nandaṇa-saricchaṁ

tatth’ujjāṇa-gihammī nāṇāviha-rūva-kamma-ṇijjuttā

dhuttāṇa sayāṇēgā samāgayā kaha vi hiṇḍantā

māyā-ṇiyaḍi-pahāṇā akajja-ṇicc’ujjuyā niraṇukampā

thī-bāla-vuḍḍha-vīsattha-ghāiṇō vañcaṇ’ēkkaparā

dhūv’añjaṇa-jōgēhi ya ōsōyaṇi-thambhaṇīhiṁ vijjāhiṁ

sara-vaṇṇa-bhēya-kusalā tēsi pahū pañc’ imē kamasō

mūlasiri kaṇḍarīō ēlāsāḍhō sasō ya khaṇḍavaṇā

ēkk’ēkkassa ya tēsiṁ dhuttāṇaṁ pañca-pañcasayā

dhuttīṇaṁ pañcasayā khaṇḍavaṇāē ya navari parivārō

savvāṇa vi tāṇa pahū mūlasirī lōya-vikkhāō

vaṭṭaï ya tāṇa kālō jalaya-samucchanna-sūra-sasi-maggō

indadhaṇu-vijju-gajjiya-jalayara-muhalō varisayālō

sattāha-duddiṇammi ya samantaō bhariya-vappa-kūva-sarē

kaya-cikkhilla-cilipphila-dussañcārāsu racchāsu

ēyārisammi kālē tē dhuttā sīya-vaddalābhihayā

bhukkh’attā bēnti tahiṁ kō amhaṁ dējja bhattaṁ ti

aha bhaṇaï mūladēvō jaṁ jēṇa suyaṁ va samaṇubhūyaṁ vā

sō taṁ kahēu savvaṁ miliyāṇ’ ēyāṇa dhuttāṇaṁ

jō taṁ na pattiējjā asacca-vayaṇaṁ ti tēṇa savvēsiṁ

dhuttāṇa samuiyāṇaṁ dāyavvaṁ bhatta-pāṇaṁ ti

jō puṇa purāṇa-bhāraha-rāmāyaṇa-sui-samuttha-vayaṇēhiṁ

taṁ vayaṇaṁ samatthittā mahāyaṇaṁ pattiyāvējja

sō dhuttāṇa ahivaī mahāmaī atthi sattha-ṇimmāō

mā dēu kiñci kassa vi iya bhaṇiē mūladēvēṇa

tē savvē viya bhaṇiyā iya hōu susōhaṇaṁ taē bhaṇiyaṁ

kintu tumaṁ paḍhamaṁ ciya kahēhi jaṁ tē samaṇubhūyaṁ

aha bhaṇaï mūladēvō jaṁ aṇubhūyaṁ maē taruṇa-bhāvē

taṁ nisuṇēha avahiyā kahijjamāṇaṁ sujuttīē

taruṇattaṇammi ahayaṁ icchiya-suha-sampayaṁ ahilasantō

dhārā-dharaṇ’aṭṭhāē sāmi-gihaṁ patthiō suiraṁ

chatta-kamaṇḍalu-hatthō panthaṁ vāhēmi gahiya-pacchayaṇō

mattaṁ pavvaya-mēttaṁ pēcchāmi ya gayavaraṁ intaṁ

mēham iva gulagulintaṁ pabhinna-karaḍā-muhaṁ mahāmattaṁ

daṭṭhūṇa vaṇagay’indaṁ bhaēṇa vēvanta-gattō haṁ

attāṇō ya asaraṇō kattha nilukkāmi haṁ ti cintantō

tō sahasā ya aïgaō kamaṇḍaluṁ maraṇa-bhayabhīō

aha sō vi matta-hatthī ūsaviya-karō sarōsa-ratt’acchō

majjhāṇumagga-laggō kamaṇḍaluṁ aïgaō sigghaṁ

tō haṁ bhaya-sambhantō samantaō vidduyaṁ palōyantō

hatthiṁ kamaṇḍalummī vāmōhēūṇa chammāsaṁ

gīvāe niggaō haṁ hatthī vi mamāṇumaggaō nintō

laggō vāl’agg’antē kuṇḍiya-gīvāe chiddammi

aham avi ya navari puraō gaṅgaṁ pēcchāmi raṅgira-taraṅgaṁ

phēṇa-ṇiyar’aṭṭahāsaṁ vaṇagaya-danta-kkhaya-taḍ’aggaṁ

ummī-sahassa-paüraṁ jhasa-mayara-ggāha-kumma-pariyariyaṁ

juvaï-hiyaya vv’ agāhaṁ uyahi vva sudūra-parapāraṁ

paham annaṁ alahantō tō haṁ isu-vēya-vāhiṇiṁ sigghaṁ

bāhāhiṁ samuttiṇṇō gōpayam iva bhārahiṁ viulaṁ

tō sāmi-gihaṁ gantuṁ chuha-taṇhā-parisahēhiṁ sahamāṇō

chammāsā sīsēṇaṁ dharēmi dhārā-dhar’aṭṭhāē

dhārēūṇa ya dhāraṁ payaō ahivandiūṇa mahasēṇaṁ

sampattō ujjēṇiṁ tubbhēhiṁ samaṁ ca miliō haṁ

tō jaï saccaṁ ēyaṁ tō mē hēūhiṁ pattiyāvēha

aha mannaha aliyaṁ taṁ dhuttāṇaṁ dēha tō bhattaṁ

aha bhaṇaï kaṇḍarīō kō bhaṇihī tumam asacca-vayaṇaṁ ti

bhāraha-purāṇa-rāmāyaṇāṇi purisō viyāṇantō

paribhaṇaï mūladēvō sō hatthī kuṇḍiyāe kaha māō

kaha bhamiō chammāsaṁ kamaṇḍalē tammi vaṇahatthī

suhuma-cchiddēṇa kamaṇḍalāo kaha niggaō ahaṁ sō ya

niggantuṁ vaṇahatthī vāl’agg’antē kahaṁ laggō

kaha gaṅgā uttiṇṇā bāhāhiṁ maē sudūra-parapārā

kaha chammāsaṁ dhariyā bhukkhiya-tisiēṇ’ udaya-dhārā

aha bhaṇaï kaṇḍarīō jaṁ summaï bhārahē purāṇē ya

taṁ jaï savvaṁ saccaṁ tō saccaṁ tujjha vi ya vayaṇaṁ

hatthī kamaṇḍalummī ahaṁ pi māō kahaṁ ti jaṁ bhaṇasi

ēttha diyāi-pasiddhaṁ vayaṇaṁ suṇa paccaya-ṇimittaṁ

bambhāṇassa muhāō vippā khattiya-jaṇō ya bāhāsu

ūrūsu niggayā kira vaïsā suddā ya pāēsu

bambhāṇassa sarīrē jaï māō ēttiō jaṇa-samūhō

tō kaha kamaṇḍalummī na māsi taṁ vaṇagaya-samaggō

annaṁ ca bambha-viṇhū uḍḍhaṁ ca ahō ya bē vi dhāvantā

antaṁ jassa na pattā vāsa-sahassēṇa divvēṇa

liṅgaṁ mahappamāṇaṁ kaha māyaṁ tass’ umā-sarīrammi

ēvaṁ jaï kuṇḍiyāē hatthī māō tti kō dōsō

annaṁ ca imaṁ suvvaï risiṇā vāsēṇa bhārahē ’bhihiyaṁ

jaha vēṇu-pavva-majjhē kīyaga-bhāiya-sayaṁ jāyaṁ

kīō vaṁsō bhaṇṇaï tahiṁ jāyā kīyaga tti bhaṇṇanti

kaha tēsiṁ uppattī virāḍa-rāy’agga-mahisīē

tīē vi natthi puttō tō sā risi-āsamammi risim ēkkaṁ

ārāhiuṁ payattā tēṇa vi sō sāhiō caruō

bhaṇiyā tēṇa ya risiṇā ēyaṁ caruyaṁ tumaṁ kuḍaṅgammi

bhuñjāhi pahiṭṭha-maṇā hōhī tē tattha putta-sayaṁ

sā taṁ paribhuttuṁ jē vaṁsa-kuḍaṅgammi tō gayā sagihaṁ

kassaï kālassa tahiṁ gaggali-ṇāmō risivarō tti

vaṁsī-jāla-paviṭṭhō caraï tavaṁ navari tattha paüma-sarē

accharasā ṇhāyantī risiṇā diṭṭhā vigaya-vatthā

khuhiyassa sukka-binduṁ paḍiyaṁ nālīe kīyagō paḍhamō

nava-ṇāya-sahassa-balō sañjāō paḍhama-bindummi

jaha jaha nijjhāi muṇī accharasaṁ pavara-rūva-lāvannaṁ

taha taha khiraï ya bīyaṁ jāyaṁ tahiṁ kīyagāṇa sayaṁ

vaṁsī-ṇālīo risī nikkhiviuṁ niyayam ālayaṁ pattō

sō cciya vaṁsa-kuḍaṅgaṁ rāyā rakkhāvaē niccaṁ

sañjāya-savva-gattā gahiyā dēvīe tuṭṭha-maṇasāē

taṁ tīē putta-sayaṁ bhaṇṇaï nālī-samuppannaṁ

jaï vaṁsa-samuppannaṁ bhāya-sayaṁ kīyagāṇa māi tahiṁ

tō kaha kamaṇḍalummī na māsi taṁ vaṇagaya-samaggō

vāsa-sahassaṁ gaṅgā jaḍāsu vāmōhiyā jaï harēṇaṁ

chammāsaṁ kuṇḍiyāē vimōhiō vaṇagaō saccaṁ

laggō vāl’agg’antē hatthī ahayaṁ ca niggaō kaha nu

jaṁ bhaṇasi taṁ suṇējjasu purāṇa-sui-āgayaṁ vayaṇaṁ

viṇhū jagassa kattā sō kira ēg’aṇṇavammi lōgammi

āgāsa-bhūmi-māruya-tasa-thāvara-jalaṇa-rahiyammi

naṭṭha-ṇarāsura-tiriē tārā-sasi-sūra-virahiē lōē

tappaï tavaṁ aṇantō jala-sayaṇa-gaō acint’appā

tassa kira paṅkaya-tthō bambhō nāhīe paüma-gabbhāō

daṇḍa-kamaṇḍalu-hatthō viṇiggaō paṅkayaṁ laggaṁ

ēvaṁ kamaṇḍalu-gīvāe niggaō jaï tumaṁ gaya-samaṁ pi

hatthī vāl’agg’antē laggō tō ēttha kim ajuttaṁ

jaṁ pi ya bhaṇasi guṇāgara kuṇḍiya-gīvāe niggaō kim ahaṁ

bhāraha-pasiddham ēttha vi vayaṇaṁ suṇa paccaya-ṇimittaṁ

divvaṁ vāsa-sahassaṁ bambhāṇō tappaī tavaṁ raṇṇē

tō khuhiyā tiyasa-gaṇā bhaṇanti kaha hōjja sē vigghaṁ

tō bhaṇaï suravar’indō thī-lōlō pasuvaī-vivāhammi

aggīkamma-ṇiuttō daṭṭhūṇa umaṁ lhasiya-cēlaṁ

khuhiō bīya-ṇisaggaṁ karēi vihuṇaï niyaṁsaṇaṁ tāhē

paḍiyaṁ bīyaṁ kalasē dōṇāyariō samuppannō

ērisiya-itthiyāhiṁ ēsō khubbhējja rūva-kaliyāhiṁ

kō itthīhiṁ na khuhiō muttūṇa jiṇaṁ mahāvīraṁ

gōyama-vasiṭṭha-pārāsarō ya jamayaggi-kāsavō-’gatthī

ahayaṁ ca surāhivaī harō harī tihuyaṇa-variṭṭhā

annē vi ēvam āī tava-satta-ṇihī jayammi vikkhāyā

vammaha-bāṇābhihayā itthīhiṁ karāviyā āṇaṁ

tā gantūṇaṁ sigghaṁ tilōttamā uttamā sura-vahūṇaṁ

khōhēu tava-visiṭṭhaṁ iṭṭhēṇa piyāmahaṁ vihiṇā

tō sā saṅgaya-vēsā tiyasāhiva-vayaṇa-gahiya-sandēsā

gantuṁ vibhussa puraō divvaṁ naṭṭaṁ payāsēi

jahaṇ’uvvahaṇa-bhamirayā ucchāliya-hāra-manthar’ucchōhā

ghaṇa-masiṇ’aṁsiya-thaṇahara-m-ukkampiya-pēlava-dugullā

laliya-paṇay’aṅgalaṭṭhī sakaḍakkh’ubbhēya-bhuya-layā-juyalā

rēhaï paṇaccamāṇī pavaṇēriya-campaya-laya vva

daṭṭhūṇa naccamāṇiṁ bambhā ēg’indiō vva saṁvuttō

nijjhāi aṇimis’acchō sēs’indiya-mukka-vāvārō

nāūṇa tassa bhāvaṁ tō sā saṅgaya-maṇōhara-suvēsā

vivihāharaṇa-vihūsā dakkhiṇapāsē ṭhiyā vihuṇō

tō taṁ apēcchamāṇō bambhāṇō maṇasi maṇasijābhihaō

taṁ daṭṭhūṇa sataṇhō karēi bīyaṁ taō vayaṇaṁ

ēttō ’varēṇa taïyaṁ caütthayaṁ kuṇaï uttarēṇaṁ sō

uḍḍhaṁ uppaïyāē pañcama-vayaṇaṁ uvari-huttaṁ

tō tassa pamattassa ya tilōttamāgaya-maṇassa ruddēṇaṁ

taṁ vayaṇaṁ pañcamayaṁ nahēṇa ukkhuṇṭiyaṁ sahasā

tō bambhā parikuviō dāhiṇa-hattha-ppaēsiṇīē u

uvvaṭṭēi nilāḍē asarisa-rōs’uṭṭhiyaṁ sēyaṁ

tatth’ uppannō sēē balavantō sēyakuṇḍalī nāma

bambhēṇ’ āṇattēṇaṁ ukkhittō saṅkarō tēṇaṁ

tō sō bhaya-sambhantō nāsantō bayariyāsamē pattō

tattha harī niyamatthō bhikkhaṁ dēhi tti saṁlattō

tā tēṇa takkhaṇaṁ ciya mukka-sirā appaṇō nilāḍammi

bambhāṇa-sira-kavālaṁ ruddēṇa samuḍḍiyaṁ hēṭṭhē

vāsa-sahassēṇa vi taṁ na bharijjaï tīe ruhira-dhārāē

taṁ ruhiram aṅgulīē pasuvaïṇā ḍōhiyaṁ navariṁ

bambhāṇa-sira-kavālē kēsava-ruhir’aṅgulīe ruddassa

tō rattakuṇḍaliṇarō tiṇhaṁ sañjōgaō jāō

sō ruddēṇ’ āṇattō pajujjhiō sēyakuṇḍalīe samaṁ

jujjhantāṇaṁ tāṇaṁ vāsa-sahassaṁ aïkkantaṁ

tō gēṇhiūṇa dōnni vi savvēhi suravarēhiṁ miliēhiṁ

dinnō sakkassa narō sūrassa samappiō bīō

bhaṇiyā bhāraha-kālē bhāraha-jujjhassa kāraṇ’aṭṭhāē

bharah’avayāra-kālē uvaṇijjaha maṇuya-lōyammi

tō kālē sampattē sūrō kuntīe rūva-ummattō

kaya-sañjōō tīē kucchīe jaṇēi taṁ gabbhaṁ

sannaddha-baddha-kavaō kuntī-kaṇṇēṇa niggaō kaṇṇō

tō kiṁ tumaṁ na nīsarasi kuṇḍi-gīvāe mūlasirī

gaṅgā aṇōra-pārā kaham uttiṇṇa tti bhaṇasi mē jaṁ tu

ēttha vi paccaya-jaṇaṇaṁ suṇēhi rāmāyaṇē vittaṁ

sīyā-paütti-hēuṁ pavaṇa-suō rāhavēṇa āṇattō

laṅkā-puriṁ aïgaō bāhāhiṁ mahōyahiṁ tariuṁ

diṭṭhāē sīyāē piya-paḍivattiṁ suṇittu tuṭṭhāē

bhaṇiō kaha tē uyahī tiṇṇō haṇuyanta sō bhaṇaï

bhavati cātra ślōkaḥ:

tava prasādāt tava ca prasādād bhartuś ca tē dēvi tava prasādāt

sādhūnatē yēna pituḥ prasādāt tīrṇō mayā gōṣpadavat samudraḥ

jaï tēṇa samuttiṇṇō tiriēṇa mahōyahī duruttārō

tō kiṁ taē nar’uttama na hōjja gaṅgā samuttiṇṇā

jaṁ bhaṇasi kaha nu dhārā chammāsā dhāriyā sirēṇa maē

ēttha vi mē suṇa hēuṁ diyāi-sui-āgayaṁ vayaṇaṁ

lōga-hiy’aṭṭhāē kira gaṅgā abbhatthiyā suravarēhiṁ

avayarasu maṇuya-lōyaṁ saggāō bhaṇaï sā tāhē

kō maṁ dhariuṁ sakkō nivaḍantiṁ bhaṇaï pasuvaī ahayaṁ

dhārēmi taō paḍiyā dhariyā sīsēṇa pasuvaïṇā

divvaṁ vāsa-sahassaṁ jaï dhariyā jaṇhavī umā-vaïṇā

tō kaha na dharēsi tumaṁ chammāsaṁ sirēṇ’ udaya-dhārā

uttama-purisō si tumaṁ vinnāṇ’āgama-guṇēhiṁ sampuṇṇō

niggaya-jasō mahappā vikkhāō jīva-lōyammi

aïsaïō mūlasirī kaṇḍariyaṁ bhaṇaï bhaṇasu ēttāhē

jaṁ diṭṭhaṁ jaṁ ca suyaṁ aṇuhūyaṁ jaṁ ca tē ihaïṁ

aha bhaṇaï kaṇḍarīō aviṇaya-puṇṇō mi āsi bālattē

ammā-pii-duddantō rōsēṇa gharāo nikkhantō

parihiṇḍantō ya ahaṁ pattō dēs’anta-saṇṭhiyaṁ gāmaṁ

gō-mahisa-ajā-ēlaya-khara-karaha-samāulaṁ muiyaṁ

ārām’ujjāṇa-vaṇēhiṁ sōhiyaṁ kusuma-phala-samiddhēhiṁ

ayalāpuri-sāricchaṁ bahu-ghara-saya-saṅkulaṁ rammaṁ

tassa bahumajjha-dēsē pēcchaṁ vaḍapāyavaṁ maṇ’abhirāmaṁ

mēha-ṇiuramba-bhūyaṁ saüṇa-sahassāṇa āvāsaṁ

tassa ya hēṭṭhē jakkhō kamaladal’akkhō guṇēhiṁ parikiṇṇō

sannihiya-pāḍihērō dēi varaṁ sō vara-tthīṇaṁ

jakkhassa tassa jattā vaṭṭaï bahujaṇa-samāulā muiyā

tatth’ ēi jaṇō muiō dhūva-balī-puppha-hattha-gaō

ṇhāya-pasāhiya-jimiō savvālaṅkāra-bhūsiya-sarīrō

nāṇāviha-vattha-dharō candaṇa-parivaṇṇaga-vilittō

tō haṁ sakōuhallō uvāgaō taṁ mahāyaṇa-samūhaṁ

jakkhassa kamē namiuṁ ramamāṇē ghōḍahē pēcchaṁ

sannaddha-baddha-kavayā gahiy’āuha-paharaṇā ya aïbahulā

kalayala-ravaṁ karēntā paḍiyā cōrā navari tattha

tō sō sabāla-vuḍḍhō sa-itthiō jaṇavaō sapasuvaggō

aha ghōḍaēhiṁ sahiō vāluṅkaṁ aïgaō savvō

tattha vi tē ghōḍahaē ramanta-pēcchā jaṇē ya muiya-maṇē

cōrā vi paḍiṇiyattā naṭṭhō gāmō tti jampantā

navari tahiṁ pasuvaggō caramāṇō āgaō suvīsatthō

ēkkāe pasūe taō ōiliyaṁ cibbhaḍaṁ sahasā

sā ayagarēṇa ghatthā sō vi ya ḍhēṅkīe navari ōiliō

sā tatth’ ēva nilīṇā tuṅgē vaḍapāyavē viulē

tassa ahē khandhārō navari ṭhiō rāiṇō ya matta-gaō

ḍhēṅkī-pāē jamiō vaḍa-pārōhō tti kāūṇa

āuñciyammi pāē kaḍḍhijjaï gayavarō gulagulintō

tō ravaï mēṇṭha-vaggō kēṇāvi gaō samukkhittō

sōūṇa tāṇa saddaṁ sampattā saddavēhiṇō jōhā

isu-cāva-gahiya-hatthā kalayala-rāvaṁ karēmāṇā

chinnā ya tīe paṅkhā sē sīsaṁ hatthiēhiṁ dakkhēhiṁ

sā vilavantī paḍiyā pavvaya-sihara vva mahi-vaṭṭhē

phālāviyā ya rannā pōṭṭē diṭṭhō ya ayagarō viulō

sagaḍassa īdarō viva khōḍī viva mahiyalē paḍiyā

aha bhaṇaï nara-var’indō phālijjaü ēsa ayagarō viulō

ēyassa vi mā majjhē māṇusa-tiriyaṁ ca hōjjāhi

aha phāliyammi uyarē diṭṭhā sā chāliyā mahākāyā

tīē vi uyara-majjhē ramaṇijjaṁ cibbhaḍaṁ diṭṭhaṁ

hī hī ahō mahallaṁ ti cibbhaḍaṁ jāva jampaē rāyā

tō ghōḍayā vi ramiuṁ navari ṭhiy’āōjja-vaṁsa-karā

niggantuṁ ca pavattō vālukkāō taō jaṇa-samūhō

jaha salabhāṇa ya sēṇā rēppha-bilāō viṇikkhamaï

namiūṇa nara-var’indaṁ tō sō sacaüppaō jaṇō savvō

niya-ṇiya-ṭhāṇāiṁ gaō ahaṁ pi pattō imaṁ nayariṁ

ēyaṁ mē aṇubhūyaṁ paccakkham ih’ ēva māṇusē lōē

jō mē na pattiyāyaï dhuttāṇaṁ dēu sō bhuttiṁ

jaha jāyā sēlasuyā vikkhāyā jīva-lōyammi

jaï hōnti pavvayāṇaṁ puttā dhūyā kuḍumba-dhammō vā

tō taṁ imammi lōē jambūdīvē na māējjā

ēyāiṁ capphalāiṁ bhāraha-rāmāyaṇē nibaddhāiṁ

sañcālaṇam asahantā jaha jutti-kayaṁ suvaṇṇa vva

ēyaṁ lōiya-satthaṁ gaddaha-liṇḍa vva bāhirē maṭṭhaṁ

jāv’ antaṁ jōijjaï tusa-bhusa-busa-mīsiyaṁ savvaṁ

tō tē bhaṇāmi savvē kusamaya-kussui-pahēṇa muttūṇa

savvanna-dēsiyammi ya laggaha maggē payattēṇaṁ

ēyaṁ dhuttakkhāṇaṁ sōūṇaṁ lōiyassa param’atthaṁ

taha kuṇaha nicchiya-maïṁ jaha darisaṇa-suddhi hōi parā

cittaüḍa-dugga-siri-saṇṭhiēhiṁ sammatta-rāya-rattēhiṁ

sucariya-samūha-sahiyā kahiyā ēsā kahā suvarā

sammatta-suddhi-hēuṁ cariyaṁ haribhadda-sūriṇā raïyaṁ

nisuṇanta-kahantāṇaṁ bhava-virahaṁ kuṇaü bhavvāṇaṁ

sēyambara-vara-sūrī haribhaddō kuṇaü amha bhaddāiṁ

jassa sasi-saṅkha-dhavalē jiṇ’āgamē ērisā bhattī
