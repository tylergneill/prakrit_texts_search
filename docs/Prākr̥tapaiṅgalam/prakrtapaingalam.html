magaṇa ṇagaṇa dui mitta hō bhagaṇa yagaṇa hō bhicca ।

āsīṇa jata duaü gaṇa avasiṭṭhaü ari ṇicca ॥

magaṇa riddhi thirakajja yagaṇa suhasampaa dijjaï ।

ragaṇa maraṇa sampalaï jagaṇa kharakiraṇa visajjaḍa ॥

tagaṇa suṇṇa phala kahaï sagaṇa sahadēsu vvāsaï

bhagaṇa racaï maṅgala aṇēka kaï piṅgala bhāsaï ॥

jata kavva gāha dōhaï muṇahu ṇagaṇa hōi paḍhamakkharahi ।

tasu riddhi buddhi savvaī phurahi raṇa rāula duttara taraï ॥

mitta mitta dē riddhi buddhi aru maṅgala dijjaï

mitta bhicca thirakajja jujjha ṇibbhaa jaa kijjaï ।

mitta āsē kajjabandha ṇahi puṇu puṇu chijjaï

mitta hōi jaï sattu gōttabandhava pīḍijjaï ॥

aru bhicca mitta sava kajjahō bhicca bhicca āatti cala

sava bhicca uāsē dhaṇu ṇasaï bhicca vaïri hākanda phala ॥

uāsīṇa jaï mitta kajja kichu manda dikhāvaï

uāsīṇa jaï bhicca savva āatti calāvaï ।

uāsīṇa uāsē mandabhala kichu ṇahi dakkhia

āsīṇa jaï sattu gōtta vaïriu kaï lakkhia ।

jaï sattu mitta hō suṇṇaphala sattu bhiccahō gharaṇiṇasa

puṇu sattu āsē dhaṇa ṇasaï sattu sattu ṇāakka khasa ॥

puvva juala sari aṅkā dijjasu । gurusira aṅkē sēsa maṭijjasu ।

uvaralaaṅka lakkhi kahu āṇahu । tē paridhua uddiṭṭhā jāṇahu ॥

ṇaḍhē savvakalā kārijjasu । puvva juala sari aṅkā dijjasu ।

pucchalaaṅka miṭāvahi sēkha । uvarala aṅka lōpi kahu lēkha ॥

jattha jattha pāvijjaï bhāga । ēhu kahaï phura piṅgala ṇāga ।

paramattā lēi gurutā jāi । jata lakkhahu tata lakkhahu āi ॥

akkhara uppari duṇṇā aṅkā dijjahu muṇēhu uddiṭṭhā ।

lahu uppari jō aṅkō taṁ dēi ēkkēṇa jāṇēhu ॥

ṇaḍhē aṅkē bhāga karijjasu । samabhāgahĩ tahi lahu mūṇijjasu ।

visama ēka dēi vaṁṭaṇa kijjasu । piṅgala jampaï guru āṇijjasu ॥

akkhara saṅkhē kāḍha karu āi anta paḍhamaka ।

sira dui aṅkē avaru bharu sūī mēru ṇisaṅka ॥

udiṭhṭhā sari aṅkā dijjasu । puvva aṅka parabharaṇa karijjasu ।

pāvala aṅka paḍhama paridijjasu । pattharasaṅkha patākā kijjasu ॥

dui dui kāṭṭhā sari lihahu paḍhama aṅka tasu anta ।

tasu āihi puṇa ēkka saü paḍhamē bē vi milanta ॥

sira aṅkē tasu sira para aṅkē । uvarala kāṭṭa purahu ṇīsakē ।

mattāmēru aṅka sañcāri । bujjhahu bujjhahu jaṇa dui cāri ॥

gāhiṇī a bāsaṭṭi kijjaï ।

taha vi palaṭṭaï siṁhiṇī bē aggala hō sahi ।

sattarūa aṇṇōṇṇa gaṇa khandha matta caüsaṭṭhi ॥

caṁḍēsaravarakittī jāva ṇa appaṁ ṇidaṁsēi ॥

jaha paḍhamaṁ taha tīaṁ dahapañca vihūsiā gāhā ॥

jēṇa viṇā ṇa jivijjaï aṇuṇijjaï sō kaāvarāhō vi ।

pattē vi ṇaaraḍāhē bhaṇa kassa ṇa vallahō aggī ॥

sattagaṇā dīhantā jō ṇa lahū chaṭṭha ṇēha jō visamē ।

taha gāhē vi a addhē chaṭhē lahuaṁ viāṇēhu ॥

savvāē gāhāē sattāvaṇṇāi hōti mattāī।

puvvaddhami a tīsā sattāīsā paradami ॥

hāsaï ēvaṁ ēkkaṁ vaṇṇaṁ tā kuṇaha ṇāmāī ॥

lacchī riddhī buddhī lajjā vijjā khamā a dēhīā ।

gōrī dhāī cuṇṇā chāā kantī mahāmāī ॥

kittī siddhī māṇī rāmā gāhiṇī visā a vāsīā ।

sōhā hariṇī cakkī sārasi kurarī sihī a haṁsīā ॥

tēraha lahuā vippī ēāīsēhiṁ khattiṇī bhaṇiā ।

sattāīsā vēsī sēsā sā suddiṇī hōi ॥

jā paḍhama tīa pañcama sattama ṭhāṇē ṇa hōi gurumajjhā ।

guvviṇiē guṇarahiā gāhā dōsaṁ paāsēi ॥

pacchima dalē ṇa tīsā ia jampia piṅgalēṇa ṇāēṇa ॥

parihara māṇiṇi māṇaṁ pakkhahi kusumāī ṇīvassa ।

tumha kaē kharahiaō gaṇhaï guḍiādhaṇuṁ a kira kāmō ॥

puvvaddhē uttaddhē mattā tīsanti suhaa sambhaṇiā।

sō uggāhō vuttō piṅgala kaï diṭṭha saṭṭi mattaṅgō ॥

sōūṇa jassa ṇāmaṁ aṁsū ṇaaṇāī sumuhi rundhati ।

bhaṇa vīra cēivaïṇō pakkhāmi muhaṁ kahaṁ jahicchaṁ sē ॥

puvvaddha tīsa mattā piṅgala pabhaṇēi muddhiṇi suṇēhi ।

uttaddhē battīsā gāhiṇi vivarīa siṁhiṇī bhaṇu saccaṁ ॥

muñcahi sundari pāaṁ appahi hasiūṇa sumuhi khaggaṁ mē ।

kappia mēcchaśarīraṁ pacchaḍa vaaṇāīṁ tumha dhua hammīrō ॥

varisaï kaṇaaha viḍhēi tappaï bhuaṇē diāṇisaṁ jaggantō।
