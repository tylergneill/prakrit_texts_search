piṇḍē uggama uppāyaṇēsaṇā jōyaṇā pamāṇē ya

iṅgāla dhūma kāraṇa aṭṭhavihā piṇḍanijjutti

piṇḍa nikāya samūhē sampiṇḍaṇa piṇḍaṇā ya samavāē

samusaraṇa nicaya uvacaya caē ya jummē ya rāsī ya

piṇḍassa u nikkhēvō caukkaō chakkaō va kāyavvō

nikkhēvaṁ kāūṇaṁ parūvaṇā tassa kāyavvā

kulaē ya caubhāgassa sambhavō chakkaē cauṇhaṁ ca

niyamēṇa sambhavō atthi chakkagaṁ nikkhivē tamhā

nāmaṇṭhavaṇāpiṇḍō davvē khettē ya kāla bhāvē ya

ēsō khalu piṇḍassa u nikkhēvō chavvihō hōi

goṇṇaṁ samayakayaṁ vā jaṁ vāvi havejja tadubhaēṇa kayaṁ

taṁ binti nāmapiṇḍaṁ ṭhavaṇāpiṇḍaṁ aō vocchaṁ

guṇanipphannaṁ goṇṇaṁ taṁ cēva jahatthama satthavī benti

taṁ puṇa khavaṇō jalaṇō tavaṇō pavaṇō paīvō ya

piṇḍaṇa bahudavvāṇaṁ paḍivakkhēṇāvi jattha piṇḍakkhā

sō samayakaō piṇḍō jaha suttaṁ piṇḍapaḍiyāī

jassa puṇa piṇḍavāyaṭṭhayaṁ paviṭṭhassa hōi sampattī

guḍaōyaṇapiṇḍēhiṁ taṁ tadubhayapiṇḍamāhaṁsu

ubhayāirittamahavā annampi hu atthi lōiyaṁ nāma

attābhippāyakayaṁ jaha sīhagadēvadattāī

goṇṇasamayārittaṁ iṇamannaṁ vā'vi sūiyaṁ nāma

jaha piṇḍautti kīrai kassai nāmaṁ manūsassa

tullē'vi abhippāē samayapasiddhaṁ na giṇhaē lōō

jaṁ puṇa lōyapasiddhaṁ taṁ sāmaiyā uvacaranti

akkhē varāḍaē vā kaṭṭhē putthē va cittakammē vā

sabbhāvamasabbhāvē ṭhavaṇāpiṇḍaṁ viyāṇāhi

ikkō u asabbhāvē tiṇhaṁ ṭhavaṇā u hōi sabbhāvē

cittēsu asabbhāvē dārualeppōvalē siyarō

tivihō u davvapiṇḍō saccittō mīsaō acittō ya

ekkekkassa ya ettō nava nava bhēā u pattēyaṁ ।। gāhā-16

puḍhavī āukkāō tēū vāū vaṇassaī cēva

bēindiya tēindiya caurō pañcendiyā cēva

puḍhavīkāō tivihō saccittō mīsaō ya accittō

saccittō puṇa duvihō nicchayavavahāraō cēva

nicchayaō saccittō puḍhavimahāpavvayāṇa bahumajjhē

accittamīsavajjō sēsō vavahārasaccittō

khīradumaheṭṭha panthē kaṭṭhōlē indhaṇē ya mīsō u

pōrisi ēga duga tigaṁ bahuindhaṇamajjhathōvē ya

sīuṇha-khāra-khattē aggī-lōṇūsa ambilē-nēhē

vukkantajōṇiēṇaṁ payōyaṇaṁ tēṇimaṁ hōi

avaraddhigavisabandhē lavaṇēṇa va surabhiuvalaēṇaṁ vā

accittassa u gahaṇaṁ paōyaṇaṁ tēṇimaṁ ca'nnaṁ

ṭhāṇanisiyaṇatuyaṭṭaṇa uccārāī cēva ussaggō

ghaṭṭagaḍagalagalēvō ēmāi paōyaṇaṁ bahuhā

āukkāō tivihō saccittō mīsaō ya accittō

saccittō puṇa duvihō nicchayavavahāraō cēva

ghanaudahī ghanavalayā karagasamuddaddahāṇa bahumajjhē

aha nicchayasaccittō vavahāranayassa aghaḍāī

usiṇōdagamanuvattē daṇḍē vāsē ya paḍiyamittammi

mottūṇādēsatigaṁ cāulaudagē'bahu pasannaṁ

bhaṇḍagapāsavilaggā uttēḍā bubbuyā na saṁmanti

jā tāva mīsagaṁ tandulā ya rajjhanti jāva'nnē

ēē u anāēsā tinni vi kālaniyamassa'sambhavaō

lukkhēyara bhaṇḍaga pavaṇa sambhavāsambhavāīhiṁ

jāva na bahuppasannaṁ tā mīsaṁ ēsa ittha āēsō

hōi pamāṇamacittaṁ bahuppasannaṁ tu nāyavvaṁ

sīuṇha khārakhattē aggī lōṇūsa ambilē nēhē

vukkantajōṇiēṇaṁ paōyaṇaṁ tēṇimaṁ hōi

parisēyapiyaṇahatthāi dhōvaṇaṁ cīradhōvaṇaṁ cēva

āyamaṇa bhāṇadhuvaṇaṁ ēmāi paōyaṇaṁ bahuhā

uubaddha dhuvaṇa bāusa bambhavināsō aṭhāṇaṭhavaṇaṁ ca

sampāimavāuvahō pāvaṇa bhūōvaghāō ya

aibhāra cuḍaṇa paṇaē sīyalapāuraṇa'jīra gēlaṇṇē

ōhāvaṇa kāyavahō vāsāsu adhōvaṇē dōsā ।। gāhā-33

appattecciya vāsē savvaṁ uvahiṁ dhuvanti jayaṇāē

asaīē u davassa ya jahannaō pāyanijjōgō

āyariyagilāṇāṇa ya mailā mailā puṇō'vi dhōvanti

mā hu gurūṇa avaṇṇō lōgammi ajīraṇaṁ iyarē

pāyassa paḍōyārō dunisijja tipaṭṭa potti rayaharaṇaṁ

ēē u na vīsāmē jayaṇā saṅkāmaṇā dhuvaṇaṁ

pāyassa paḍōyārō pattagavajjō ya pāyanijjōgō

donni nisijjāō puṇa abbhintara bāhirā cēva

santhāruttaracōlagapaṭṭā tinni u havanti nāyavvā

muhapottiyatti pottī ēganisejjaṁ ca rayaharaṇaṁ

ēē u na vīsāmē paidinamuvaōgaō ya jayaṇāē

saṅkāmiūṇa dhōvanti vijja chappaiyā tattha vihiṇā u

jō puṇa vīsamijjai taṁ ēvaṁ vīyarāya-āṇāē

pattē dhōvaṇakālē uvahiṁ vīsāmaē sāhū

abbhintaraparibhōgaṁ uvariṁ pāuṇai nāidūrē ya

tinni ya tinni ya ēgaṁ nisiṁ tu kāuṁ paricchijjā

dhōvatthaṁ tinni dinā uvariṁ pāuṇai taha ya āsannaṁ

dhārēi tinni diyahē ēgadinaṁ uvari lambantaṁ

kēī ekkekkanisiṁ saṁvāsēuṁ tihā paricchanti

pāuṇai jai na lagganti chappaiyā tāhi dhōvanti

nivvōdagassa gahaṇaṁ kēī bhāṇēsu asui paḍisēhō

gihibhāyaṇēsu gahaṇaṁ ṭhiya vāsē mīsagaṁ chārō

gurupaccakkhāṇigilāṇa sēhamāīṇa dhōvaṇaṁ puvvaṁ

tō appaṇō puvvamahākaḍē ya iyarē duvē pacchā

acchōḍapiṭṭaṇāsu ya na dhuvē dhōē payāvaṇaṁ na karē

paribhōga aparibhōgē chāyāyava pēha kallāṇaṁ

tivihō tēukkāō saccittō mīsaō ya accittō

sacittō puṇa duvihō nicchaya vavahāraō cēva

iṭṭagapāgāīṇaṁ bahumajjhē vijjumāi nicchayaō

iṅgālāī iyarotti mummuramāī u missō u

ōyaṇavañjaṇapānaga āyāmusiṇōdagaṁ ca kummāsā

ḍagalagasarakkhasūī pippalamāī u uvaōgō

vāukkāō tivihō saccittō mīsaō ya accittō

sacittō puṇa duvihō nicchayavavahāraō cēva ।। gāhā-50

savalaya ghanatanuvāyā aihima aiduddiṇē ya nicchayaō

vavahāra pāiṇāī akkantāī ya accittō

akkantadhantaghāṇē dēhānugaē ya pīliyāisu ya

accitta vāukāō va ēvavihō bhaṇiō kammaṭṭhamahaṇēhiṁ

hatthasayamēga gantā daiō accittu bīyaē mīsō

taiyammi u sacittō vatthī puṇa pōrisidinēsu

niddhēyarō ya kālō ēgantisiṇiddhamajjhimajahannō

lukkhōvi hōi tivihō jahannamajjhō ya ukkōsō

ēgantasiṇiddhaṁmī pōrisimēgaṁ acēaṇō hōi

biiyāē saṁmīsō taiyāi sacēyaṇō vatthī

majjhimaniddhē dō pōrisīu accittu mīsaō taiē

cautthīē saccittō pavaṇō daiyāi majjhagaō

pōrisitigamaccittō niddhajahannammi mīsaga cautthī

saccitta pañcamīē ēvaṁ lukkhē'vi dinavuḍḍhī

daiēṇa vatthiṇā vā paōyaṇaṁ hojja vāuṇā muṇiṇō

gēlannammi va hojjā sacittamīsē pariharejjā

vaṇasaikāō tivihō saccittō mīsaō ya accittō

saccittō puṇa duvihō nicchayavavahāraō cēva

savvō'va'nantakāō saccittō hōi nicchayanayassa

vavahārassa ya sēsō mīsō pavvāyaroṭṭāī

pupphāṇaṁ pattāṇaṁ saraḍuphalāṇaṁ tahēva hariyāṇaṁ

veṇṭammi milāṇammi nāyavvaṁ jīvavippajaḍhaṁ

santhārapāyadaṇḍagakhōmiya kappā ya pīḍhaphalagāī

ōsahabhēsajjāṇi ya ēmāi paōyaṇaṁ bahuhā

biyatiyacaurō pañcandiyā ya tippabhii jattha u samenti

saṭṭhāṇē saṭṭhāṇē sō piṇḍō tēṇa kajjamiṇaṁ

bēindiyaparibhōgō akkhāṇa sasippasaṅkhamāīṇaṁ

tēindiyāṇa uddēhigādi jaṁ vā vaē vejjō

caurindiyāṇa macchiyaparihārō cēva āsamakkhiyā cēva

pañcendiyapiṇḍammi u avvavahārī u nēraiyā

cammaṭṭhidantanaharōmasiṅga avilāichagaṇagōmuttē

khīradadhimāiyāṇa ya pañcindiyatiriyaparibhōgō

saccittē pavvāvaṇa panthuvaēsē ya bhikkhadāṇāī

sīsaṭṭhiga accittē mīsaṭṭhisarakkhapahapucchā ।। gāhā-67

khamagāi kālakajjāiēsu pucchijja dēvayaṁ kañci

panthē subhāsubhē vā pucchēī divva uvaōgō

aha mīsaō ya piṇḍō ēēsiṁ ciya navaṇha piṇḍāṇaṁ

duga sañjōgāīō nāyavvō jāva caramotti

sōvīra gōrasāsava vēsaṇa bhēsajja nēha sāgaphalē

poggalalōṇa gulōyaṇa nēgā piṇḍā u sañjōgē

tinni u paēsasamayā ṭhāṇaṭṭhiiu daviē tayāēsā

caupañcamapiṇḍāṇaṁ jattha jayā tapparūvaṇayā

muttadaviēsu jujjai jai annonnāṇuvēhaō piṇḍō

muttivimuttēsuvi sō jujjai nanusaṅkhabāhullā

jaha tipaēsō khandhō tisuvi paēsēsu jō samōgāḍhō

avibhāgiṇa sambaddhō kahannu nēvaṁ tadādhārō?

ahavā cauṇha niyamā jōgavibhāgēṇa jujjaē piṇḍō

dōsu jahiyaṁ tu piṇḍō vaṇṇijjai kīraē vāvi

duvihō u bhāvapiṇḍō pasatthaō cēva appasatthō ya

ēēsiṁ dōṇhampiya pattēya parūvaṇaṁ vocchaṁ

ēgavihāidasavihō pasatthaō cēva appasatthō ya

sañjama vijjācaraṇē nāṇāditigaṁ ca tivihō u

nāṇaṁ daṁsaṇaṁ tava sañjamō ya vaya pañca chacca jāṇejjā

piṇḍēsaṇaṁ pāṇēsaṇaṁ uggahapaḍimā ya piṇḍammi

pavayaṇamāyā nava bambhaguttiō taha ya samaṇadhammō ya

ēsa pasatthō piṇḍō bhaṇiō kammaṭṭhamahaṇēhiṁ

apasatthō ya asañjama annāṇaṁ avirai ya micchattaṁ

kōhāyāsavakāyā kammē'guttī a hammō ya

bajjhai ya jēṇa kammaṁ sō savvō hōi appasatthō u

muccai ya jēṇa sō uṇa pasatthaō navari vinnēō

daṁsaṇanāṇacarittāṇa pajjavā jē u jattiyā vā'vi

sō sō hōi tayakkhō pajjavapēyālaṇā piṇḍō

kammāṇa jēṇa bhāvēṇa appagaṁ ciṇai cikkaṇaṁ piṇḍaṁ

sō hōi bhāvapiṇḍō piṇḍayaē piṇḍaṇa jamhā

davvē accittēṇaṁ bhāvammi vēya pasatthaēṇihaṁ pagayaṁ

uccāriyatthasarisā sīsamai vikōvaṇaṭṭhāē

āhārauvahisejjā pasatthapiṇḍassuvaggahaṁ kuṇai

āhārē ahigārō aṭṭhahiṁ ṭhāṇēhiṁ sō suddhō ।। gāhā-84

nivvāṇaṁ khalu kajjaṁ nāṇāitigaṁ ca kāraṇaṁ tassa

nivvāṇakāraṇāṇaṁ ca kāraṇaṁ hōi āhārō

jaha kāraṇaṁ tu tantū paḍassa tēsiṁ ca honti pamhāiṁ

nāṇāitigassēvaṁ āhārō mokkhanēmassa

jaha kāraṇamanuvahayaṁ kajjaṁ sāhēi avikalaṁ niyamā

mokkhakkhamāṇi ēvaṁ nāṇāīṇi u avigalāiṁ

saṅkhēvapiṇḍiyatthō ēvaṁ piṇḍō maē samakkhāō

phuḍaviyaḍapāyaḍatthaṁ vocchāmi ēsaṇaṁ ettō

ēsaṇa gavēsaṇa maggaṇā ya uggōvaṇā ya boddhavvā

ēē u ēsaṇāē nāmā ēgaṭṭhiyā honti

nāmaṇṭhavaṇā daviē bhāvammi ya ēsaṇā muṇēyavvā

davvē bhāvē ekkekkayā u tivihā muṇēyavvā

jammaṁ ēsai ēgō suyassa annō tamēsaē naṭṭhaṁ

sattuṁ ēsai annō paēṇa annō ya sē maccuṁ

ēmēva sēsaēsu vi cauppayāpaya acittamīsēsu

jā jattha jujjaē ēsaṇā u taṁ tattha jōejjā

bhāvēsaṇā u tivihā gavēsagahaṇēsaṇā u boddhavvā

gāsēsaṇā u kamasō pannattā vīyarāgēhiṁ

agaviṭṭhassa u gahaṇaṁ na hōi na ya agahiyassa paribhōgō

ēsaṇatigassa ēsā nāyavvā ānupuvvī u

nāmaṁ ṭhavaṇā daviē bhāvammi gavēsaṇā muṇēyavvā

jiyasattudēvicitta sabhapavisaṇaṁ kanagapiṭṭhapāsaṇayā

dōhaladubbalapucchā kahaṇaṁ āṇā ya purisāṇaṁ

sīvannisarisamōyagakaraṇaṁ sīvannirukkhaheṭṭhēsu

āgamana kuraṅgāṇaṁ pasattha apasattha uvamā u

viiamēyaṁ kuraṅgāṇaṁ jayā sīvanni sīyai

purāvi vāyā vāyantā na uṇaṁ puñjakapuñjakā

accudaēṇa nalavaṇā ahirūḍhā gayakulāgamanaṁ

viiyamēyaṁ gajakulāṇaṁ jayā rōhanti nalavaṇā

annayāvi jharanti jharā na ya ēvaṁ bahuōdagā

uggama uggōvaṇa maggaṇā ya ēgaṭṭhiyāi ēyāṇi

ghaḍagasarāvēsu kayā u mōgayā laḍḍugapiyassa

jogga ajiṇṇa māruya nisagga tisamuttha tō suisamutthō

kāyavaimanō tinni u ahavā dēhāuindiyappāṇā

vahanaṁ karēi dāyā kāyēṇa tamāhakammaṁ ti

jaṁ davvaṁ udagāisu chūḍhamahē vayai jaṁ ca bhārēṇaṁ

sīīē rajjuēṇa va ōyaraṇaṁ davva'hēkammaṁ
