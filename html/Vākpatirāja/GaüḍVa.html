Gaüḍavahaṁ

by Bappaïrāō

paḍhamaṁ cia dhavala-kaōvavīamamburuha-gōaraṁ ṇamaha

hari-jaḍhara-ṇiggamukkhitta-ṇāla-suttaṁ piva sayambhuṁ

sō jaaï kaṇṇiā-valaa-gabbha-parigūḍha-viaḍa-bīammi

jō vasaï ṇihāṇīkaa-bahu-bambhaṇḍe vva kamalammi

taṁ paṇamaha tiṅgicchi-cchalēṇa ṇivasaï ṇivēsa-kamalammi

jassa paramāṇu-ṇivaho vva bhuvaṇa-ṇimmāṇa-paḍivaṇṇō

aṭṭhiamāvattantī sō jaaï javujjaassa sā jassa

phalihakkhāvali-kajjammi ghaḍaï bambhaṇḍa-parivāḍī

paṇamaha heṭṭha-ṭṭhia-viaḍa-sēsa-bisa-kanda-kandalāhintō

paḍibhiṇṇa ubbhiṇṇaṁ -kaṇha-paṅkaṁ viṇiggamaṁ viṇiggayaṁ ṇāhi-ṇaliṇassa

viaḍa-sasi-maṇḍalāanta-salila-bhariammi bhuvaṇa-valaammi

hariṇō hariṇa-cchāaṁ vilāsa-parisaṇṭhiaṁ jaaï

asurōraṭṭhi-ṇihaṁsaṇa-visaṭṭa-dhūmāvalambi-ṇaha-hīrō

rava- rōsa-gahia rōsamahiya -ghaṇa-maṇḍalo vva ṇara-kēsarī jaaï

taṁ ṇamaha samōsariā ṇahagga-vihaḍāviōra vīḍhassa vīḍhammi

jassa daïccammi aladdha-vavasiaccēa bhuadaṇḍā

ṇamaha viāria-daṇuinda-ruhira-pallavia-gaaṇa-pērantaṁ

riu-vaha-samaya-pasāria-sañjhaṁ piva mahumaha-maïndaṁ

mukkē vi ṇara-maïndattaṇammi bokkāra-kalusia-galassa

hariṇō jaaï ciraṁ vihaa-sadda-parighaggharā vāṇī

avahīria-diggaa-canda-hariṇa-palayabbha-jāla-rasiassa

asure ccia saṁrambhā jaanti māyā-maïndassa

rava-rōsa-dalia-ghaṇa-ṇiravalamba-saṅghaḍia-taḍi-kaḍappo vva

ṇara-hariṇō jaaï kaḍāra-kēsarō kandharā-bandhō

sō jaaï gōva-bhāvē jō vimalia-gūḍha-ṇāhi-ṇaliṇāhi

ṇīsaramāṇēhiṁ cēa parimalaṁ vahaï sasiēhiṁ

dāḍhā mahā-varāhassa vaaṇa-maggēṇa ṇiggaā jaaï

uara-ṭṭhia-ṇāhī-kamala-kanda-mūlāhi va muṇālī

taṁ ṇamaha jō varāhattaṇammi phaṇa-maṇi-ghaḍanta-paḍibimbō

sēsa-ṭṭhiaṁ pi vasuhaṁ vahaï vva pahāva-saṅkantō

heṭṭha-ṭṭhia heṭṭhāgaya -sūra-ṇivāraṇāa chattaṁ ahō iva vahantī

jaaï sasēsā vārāha-sāsa-dūrukkhaā puhavī

aṅgāĩ viṇhuṇō vāmaṇattaṇē visama-māsa-thauḍāiṁ

maḍahōara-ṇapahuppanta-bhuvaṇa-bhariāĩ va jaanti

jaaï dharamuddharanto bhara-ṇīsāria-muhagga-calaṇēṇa

ṇia-dēhēṇa karēṇa va pañcaṅguliṇā mahā-kummō

rakkhau vō rōma-laā māyā-mahilattaṇē mahumahassa

gūḍhōara-tāmarasāṇusāriṇī bhamara-māla vva bhamarapantivva

sō jaaï jassa juvaïttaṇammi khāmōarōsarantēhiṁ

bhuvaṇēhĩ va thaṇa-jahaṇāṇa thaṇaramaṇāṇa gāravaṁ kiṁ pi ṇivvaḍiaṁ

sō jaaï taṁ ṇamaha jāmaïllāamāṇa-muhalāli-valaa-pariālaṁ

lacchi-ṇivēsantēura-vaïṁ va jō vahaï vaṇamālaṁ

bālattaṇammi hariṇō jaaï jasōāe cumbiaṁ vaaṇaṁ

paḍisiddha-ṇāhi-magguddha-ṇiggaaṁ puṇḍarīaṁ va

ṇaha-rēhā rāhā-kāraṇāō karuṇaṁ harantu vō sarasā

vaccha-tthalammi kotthuha-kiraṇāantīō kaṇhassa

taṁ ṇamaha jēṇa ajjavi vilūṇa-kaṇṭhassa rāhuṇō valaï calaï

dukkhamaṇiccariaṁ cia amūla-lahuēhĩ mūlalahūēhiṁ sāsēhiṁ

paṇamaha balassa huṅkāra-bhaa-vasā ghōlamāṇa-jauṇe vva

maa-dōsa-siḍhila-kasaṇāvarilla-sandāṇiē calaṇē

antō-saṁlīṇa-phaṇā-sahassa-raaṇa-ppahaṁ piva vamantī

haliṇō maāruṇā viddavēu duriāiṁ vō diṭṭhī

appāṇa-gamia-viaṇattaṇēṇa bhara-dalia-sēsa-kummāṇa

ṇiruvā aṇuvā lambhaṁ bala-kēsavāṇa parivaggiaṁ jaaï

taṁ ṇamaha pīa-vasaṇaṁ jō vahaï sahāva-sāmala-cchāaṁ

diasa-ṇisā-laa-ṇiggama-vihāa-sabalaṁ piva sarīraṁ

siri-thaṇa-ṇivēsa-maggā jaanti bhiṇṇaṅgarāa-ṇivvaḍiā

vacchammi ṇāhi- ṇaliṇī paṅkaya -dalāamāṇā mahumahassa

sō jaaï jassa ṇāḍāla-lōaṇāsaṅgiṇī bhumaa-lēhā

ajja vi dīsaï kāmassa dāha- kasaṇā kasiṇā dhaṇu-laa vva

taṁ ṇamaha kāma-ṇēhā ajja vi dhārēi jō jaḍā-baddhaṁ

taïa-ṇaaṇaggi-ṇivaḍaṇa-kaa-vavasāaṁ piva miaṅkaṁ

sō vō suhāĩ uvaṇēu viaḍa-taïacchi-pelliaṁ jassa

ṇivasaï sīsammi sasi-cchalēṇa ṇiaaṁ piva ṇaḍālaṁ

kaḍhḍhia-kusuma-raukkara-kaḍāra-cāva-cchalēṇa sō jaaï

ahidhāviūṇa gilio vva jassa taïacchiṇā kāmō

mauḍucchaṅga-pariggaha-miaṅka-jōṇhāva bhāsiṇō hāsiṇō ṇamaha

ṇiccaṁ cia pasuvaïṇō pariṭṭhiaṁ vāmaṇa-cchāaṁ

kēyūrōraa-suṅkāra-saṅkulē bāṇa-bhua-vaṇa-cchēā

sō jaaï saṇīsāse vva vahaï jō amarisā bāhū

uddhaṁ andhaa-riuṇō ṇaaṇaṁ pajjalia-tāraaṁ ṇamaha

ukkarisia-paḍicakkaṁ va cakkiṇō bāṇabhaṅgammi

jamia-sihaṇḍassa vi visaharēṇa hatthō piṇāiṇō jaaï

lambi-jaḍā-bhāsa-phaṇā-raaṇa-maūhēsu ghōlantō

kuviāi calaṇa-paṇaō rahasukkhitta-maulī harō jaaï

sīsa-ṭṭhia-bambha-kavāla-phaṁsa-saṅkāe va umāē

taṁ paṇamaha ajja vi vahaï tivahaā-vīi-bhaṅga-kuḍilāiṁ

jassa jaḍā-bandha-ṇivēsa-visamiāiṁ va salilāiṁ

jaaï jaḍā-sañjamaṇaṁ pasuvaïṇō jalahi-mahaṇa-viramammi

siḍhilēṇa sēla-ṇihasaṇa- kilanta-vacchēṇa kilintadēhēṇa vāmaṇa-cchāaṁ

patthārōsaṇa-taralia-karaṅgulī-malia-tambira-ṇaḍālaṁ

paḍisiddha-taïa-ṇaaṇuggamaṁ va ṇamimō hara-kirāaṁ

sō jaaï jhatti-kavalia-visassa kaṇṭhammi jassa ṇivvaḍiā

khaṇa-diṇṇuvvatta-kaanta-pāsa-ṇihasōvamā rēhā

taṁ paṇamaha jō sīsāō kaha vi lhasia-ṭṭhiaṁ samuvvahaï

ajja vi kusumīkaa-kēsavacchi-vattaṁ va taïacchiṁ

uvari-ṭṭhia-candālōa-puñjiā jaaï kaṇṭha-mūlammi

sīsa-cchāya vva visa-ppahāmaī thāṇuṇō rēhā

dēhaddhaddha-pariṭṭhia-gōri-harārāhaṇekka-hiaaṁ va

khaṇḍattaṇēṇa paṇamaha pariṭṭhiaṁ tiṇaaṇa-miaṅkaṁ

taṁ ṇamaha jassa muha-ghaḍia-sasi-alā-suttiṇā ciraṁ pīā

sīsammi saaṇhēṇa va sura-sari-dhārā kavālēṇa

ghōlira-kaḍāra-tāraṁ ṇaḍāla-ṇaaṇaṁ piṇāiṇō ṇamaha jayaï

ajja vi khaāhuīkaa-jalanta-bambhaṇḍa-piṇḍaṁ va

taṁ ṇamaha kulaṁ piva jassa sulaha-sambhāvaṇaṁ pariharantō

ajja vi bāṇamaṇaṅgō ṇō muaï bhayā kumārammi

sā jaaï ṇēurillēṇa jīe calaṇēṇa tāḍia-kkhandhō

ubbhiṇṇa uggiṇṇa -ruhira-kusumō jāō mahisāsurāsōō

paḍhama-harāliṅgaṇa-lajjiāe ajjāe vō suhaṁ dēntu

kaṇṭha-ppahāvalambaṇa-thōa-tthāmāĩ acchīiṁ

bhamiaṁ palaya-paōsē viviha-kavālammi ṇamaha jayaï kālīē

miliāsēsa-ṇisā-lakkha-visama-bahu-canda-khaṇḍe vva

ḍimbaṁ vō cāmuṇḍāe pihu-sirā-daṇḍa-maṇḍaṇaṁ harau

maḍahōara-ṇapahuppanta-valaa-bhariaṁ piva khāmōaraṇapahuttantavalayavaliyaṁ piva sarīraṁ

sā jaaï cau-muhāsaṇa-tāmarasa-dalōarēsu dullaliā

kaï-jīhā-ṇi vahēsu a vahēsu va jā ṇimia-paaṁ paribbhamaï

taṁ ṇamaha jassa dīsaï bimbaṁ vicchāa-lañchaṇa-cchāyaṁ

gaṅgā-ṇiggama-parigalia-majjha-lāvaṇṇa-tucchaṁ va

vērulia-ṇihā taṁ ṇamaha jassa tē sandaṇaṁ parivahanti

taddiasa-paḍhama-pellia-tama-maïlaṅgā iva turaṅgā

jaaï jaa-rakkhaṇē puñjiaṁ va jaṁ phuraï paaṇuaṁ raviṇō

palayammi jaṁ ca paviralliaṁ va pavirelliyaṁ pi bahalāi gō-jālaṁ

taṁ ṇamaha kōla-tulaṇē saala-phaṇā-maṇi- pasatta-paḍibimbaṁ samattapaḍibimbaṁ ghaḍantapaḍibimbaṁ

uvvahaï va jō bahu-sira-vihatta-lahuaṁ dharā-valayaṁ

paṇamaha kālindī-salila-daṇḍa-ghaḍiaṁ va sura-sari-ppavahaṁ

danta-kkhambhaṁ vīsanta-viaḍa-hatthaṁ gaa-muhassa

taṁ ṇamaha gaa-muhaṁ viaḍa-danta-kara-daṇḍa-kalaṇa-līlāsu

milia-himavanta-viñjjhe vva kuṇaï jō mēru-pabbhārē

gaṇa-vaïṇō saï-saṅgaa-gōrī-hara-pēmma-rāa-viliassa

dantō vāma-muhaddhanta dantō baliyamuhaddhanta -puñjiō jaaï hāso vva

ṇamaha daṇuēnda- ṇihaṇē damaṇē māā-mahu-sūaṇaṁ uvēntīē

māṇia-taralattaṇa-vibbhamāĩ bhamiāĩ lacchīē

hatthālambia-paṅkaa-miaṅkamuahīō ṇiggaaṁ jaaï

āsaṅghia-ṇia-āṇaṇa-sōhagga-guṇāe va sirīē

kōsuma-dhaṇu-bāṇa-ggaha-sōraha-lagga-bhamare vva kāmassa

oppuṁsia oppusiya -raï-paṇaaṁsu-kajjalaṅkē karē ṇamaha

dēu suhaṁ vō pasu-vaï-sirāhi gōrī-visūriavvēhiṁ

sōvālambha vva himālaaṅka-parighōlirī gaṅgā

sā jaaï hara-siratthammi jīe sālilammi ghōlira-kavālō

ajja vi piāmahattamaṇahaṁ caurāṇaṇō vahaï

hari-calaṇa-ṇaha-ppahāē vicchōliaṁ va paḍhamaaṁ

hara-sasiṇō pāaēhiṁ saṁvaliaaṁ va vīaaṁ

gahia-hima-cchāaaṁ va tuhiṇaddi-samāvaḍaṇaē

kāraṇa-parisuddhaaṁ va gaṅgāe ṇamaha salilaaṁ

iha tē jaanti kaïṇō jaamiṇamō jāṇa saala-pariṇāmaṁ

vāāsu ṭhiaṁ dīsaï āmōa-ghaṇaṁ va tucchaṁ va

ṇiaāeccia vāāe attaṇō gāravaṁ ṇivēsantā nisēvantā

jē ēnti pasaṁsaṁ ccia jaanti iha tē mahā-kaïṇō

doggaccammi vi sokkhāĩ tāṇa vihavē vi hōnti ṇavara dukkhāiṁ

kavva-paramattha-rasiāĩ jāṇa jāanti hiaāiṁ

ummillaï lāyaṇṇaṁ paaa-cchāāe sakkaa-vaāṇaṁ sakkayapayāṇa

sakkaa-sakkārukkarisaṇēṇa paaassa vi pahāvō

ṭhiamaṭṭhiaṁ va dīsaï aṭhiaṁ pi pariṭṭhiaṁ va tahaṭṭhiyaṁ va paḍihāi

jaha-saṇṭhiaṁ ca dīsaï sukaīṇa imāō payaīō

viṇaa-guṇō daṇḍāḍambarō a maṇḍanti jaha ṇarinda-siriṁ

taha ṭaṅkārō mahurattaṇaṁ a vāaṁ pasāhēnti

sōhēi suhāvēi a suhāvēi va uvahujjantō lavō vi lacchīē

dēvī sarassaī uṇa asamaggā kiṁ pi kaṁ na kiṁ na viṇaḍēi

mahumaha-viaa-pauttā vāā kaha ṇāma maulau imammi

paḍhama-kusumāhi taliṇaṁ pacchā-kusumaṁ vaṇa-laāṇa

laggihii ṇa vā suaṇē vayaṇijjaṁ dujjaṇēhĩ bhaṇṇantaṁ

tāṇa puṇa taṁ suaṇāvavāa-dōsēṇa saṅghaḍaï

para-guṇa-parihāra-paramparāe taha tē guṇaṇṇuā guṇuṇṇayā jāā

jāā tēhiṁ cia jaha guṇēhĩ guṇiṇō paraṁ pisuṇā

jaṁ ṇimmalā vi khijjanti hanta vimalēhĩ sajjaṇa-guṇēhiṁ

taṁ sarisaṁ sasi-ara-kāraṇāe kari-danta-viaṇāē

jāṇa asamēhĩ vihiā jāaï ṇindā samā salāhā vi

ṇindā vi tēhĩ vihiā ṇa tāṇa maṇṇē kilāmēi

ṇandantu ṇiaa-guṇa-gāravammi addiṭṭha-para-muha-cchāā

garuā sa-sīla-dōlāamāṇa-para-diṭṭha-muha-rāā

bahuō sāmaṇṇa- maïttaṇēṇa guṇattaṇēṇa tāṇaṁ pariggahē lōō

kāmaṁ gaā pasiddhiṁ sāmaṇṇa-kaī aoccēa

suaṇa-sahāvē vi gaō jaḍammi kattō guṇō samullasaï

raviṇō vi jalammi ṭhiaṁ paḍibimbamahōmuhaṁ phuraï

haraï aṇū vi para-guṇō garuammi vi ṇia-guṇē ṇa santōsō asantōsō

sīlassa vivēassa a sāramiṇaṁ ramiyavvaṁ ettiaṁ cēa

iarē vi phuranti guṇā gurūṇa paḍhamaṁ kauttamāsaṅgā

aggē sēlagga-gaā indu-maūhā iva mahīē

ṇivvāḍantāṇa sivaṁ saalaṁ cia sivaaraṁ tahā tāṇa

ṇivvaḍaï kiṁ pi jaha tē vi appaṇā vimhaamuvēnti

pāsammi ahaṅkārī hōhii kaha vā guṇāṇa vivarukkhē

gavvaṁ ṇa guṇi-gaa-maō guṇatthamicchanti guṇa-kāmā

ahilaṅghiūṇa lōaṁ ṭhiāṇa ekkattaṇēṇa garuāṇa guruyāṇa

bīāvekkhī avalambiūṇa kaṁ maccharō phurau phuraï

ṇia-maï-sandēho ccia maï-sandēhāvalōaṇē jāṇa

hōnti viāra-ṭṭhāṇaṁ ṭhia-rūā tē ṇa lōassa

tucchā-suhēṇa vijjāe maccharāḍambaro khala-aṇassa

dukkhēṇa sajjaṇāṇaṁ hiyaukkampī samijjanti

sukaī… … …bhēsu jāṇa parisaṇṭhiāĩ ṇāmāiṁ

ṇiaa-ṇibandhēsu a tāṇa ṇaṇu samattaṁ ca kāaddhaṁ

pāṇamaïāō jāṇaṁ dharanti kavvēsu vaṇṇa-mālāō

…bhāvā jīassa tē jianticcia maā vi

kiṁ ratusamārūḍha-guṇāṇa jīviaṁ laddha-pisuṇa-bhāvaṁ vva

tēhiṁ pasaṁsijjantō vi jaṁ guṇī ṇēa pattiiaï

sā jaaï maī guruā gamaṇa-paḍibōha-kāraṇā… …।

paḍibōha-saṅkamā jā saaṁ va paḍivajjaï sarūvaṁ

jō appaṇāṇa sāraṁ pecchaï aṇṇassa sō vi pīhēi

paḍivajjaï jō vi parāō sō vi… …ottēā

guṇiṇā ahamā a ṭhiāṇa ṇavara tāṇaṁ ca do ccia visēsā

sō vi ahamo ccia kaō majjhima-sambhāvaṇā jassa

kō ṇindaï ṇīaamē garuaarē kō pasaṁsiuṁ taraï

sāmaṇṇaṁ ccia ṭhāṇaṁ thuīṇa pariṇindiāṇaṁ ca

sōūṇa muṇanti paraṁ jē ṇa viappanti appaṇaccēa

tē aṇaraha vva aṇaharavva aṇarihavva gavvassa uttuṇā kaha ṇa lajjanti

kāla-guṇā paḍhama-kaīhĩ bhamiamapariggahēsu maggēsu

iharā maīhĩ maïē maīē hīranti dukkaraṁ kē vi kāṇaṁ pi

kattō ṇāma ṇaïṭṭhaṁ aïṭṭhaṁ saccaṁ kavi-sēviēsu maggēsu

sīmantē uṇa mukkammi tammi savvaṁ ṇavaṁ cēa

jaḍabuddhīṇa pahuppaï maïlō guṇa-ṇiggamō ṇa maṇuyāṇa

ṇīlaṁ ṇa ṇahaṁ sā ṇāyaṇassa tēassa viṇivittī

atthālōaṇa-taralā iara-kaīṇaṁ bhamanti buddhīō

atthaccēa ṇirārambhamēnti hiaaṁ kaïndāṇa

ā-saṁsāraṁ kaï-puṅgavēhĩ taddiaha-gahia-sārō vi

ajja vi abhiṇṇa-muddo vva jaaï vāā-paripphandō

kiṁ aṇṇamaṇaṇṇa- maṇēhĩ maṇāhiṁ tāva su-kaīhĩ sikkhiā vāā

jāaṁ ṇīsāmaṇṇāṇa tāṇa mūattaṇaṁ jāva

kō va viārēi visēsa-vittharaṁ tāṇa jaṁ ṭhiaṁ lōē hiyaē

ṇāme ccia saṅkantā garuāṇa guṇā phuranti vva

jāṇa sarūvāvagamē vi gavviā kimpi hōnti aṇṇē vi

tē āāsāa vahanti attaṇō appaṇō ṇavara māhappaṁ

mōha-paḍibōha-kārittaṇēṇa timiraṁ va jaṁ paāso vva timiraṁ ca jaṁ payāsō ya

taṁ jaaï viruddhaṁ piva viappa-jālaṁ kaïndāṇa

ṇavamattha-daṁsaṇaṁ saṁṇivēsa-sisirāō bandha-riddhīō

aviralamiṇamō ā-bhuvaṇa-bandhamiha ṇavara paaammi

jē suṇṇā iva bahusō kahiṁ pi dīsanta-virala-guṇa-sārā

dara-vasia-purāārēhĩ tēhĩ kiṁ vā ṇibandhēhiṁ

ṇimmāā te ccia ṇavara kavva-kittīō jāṇa lōassa

kaṇṇē visanti hiaē vasanti pasaranti a muhammi

do ccia ṇavaraṁ hiaē lagganti jahā-ṇivēsa-ramaṇijjā

ramaṇīō ballahāṇaṁ mahā-kaīṇaṁ ca bhaṇiīō

saalāō imaṁ vāā visanti ettō a ṇēnti vāāō

ēnti samuddaṁ cia ṇēnti sāarāo ccia jalāiṁ
